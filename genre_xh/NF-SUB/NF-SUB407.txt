ISTAYFREE® ikunika ukhuseleko olukhethekileyo noluthambileyo lomqhaphu xa usexesheni kwaye uziva ungakhululekanga.
I-STAYFREE® Maxi Slims ezintsha ezenzelwe ngokukodwa amabhinqa zenziwe zancinci kuneephedi eziqhelekileyo kwaye zogqunywe ngomqhaphu kunjalonje zikukhusela ngokukuko. Ngoku uyakwazi ukuzithemba xa unxibe iphedi encinci eyenza umsebenzi ofanayo nalowo wenziwa yiphedi enkulu xa usexesheni. Ezinye zazo zineempiko ukanti ezinye azinazo. ZikwaneNatural Agent ephelisa ivumba elibi. Umqhaphu ezogqunywe ngawo ukhusel' ulusu lungakhuthuki ngokuluvumela luphefumle. Lo mqhaphu unezinto ezigcina iphedi yomile ize ikukhusele kamnandi ngokuthe chatha.[x]

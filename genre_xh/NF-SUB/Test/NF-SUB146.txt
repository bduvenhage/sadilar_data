IMADIKWE River Lodge iselunxwemeni lomlambokazi iGroot Marico River kumda waseBotswana kwaye ivumela abatyeleli ukuba bonwabele ubuhle bendalo base-Afrika behleli kweli bhotwe lobuncwane.
Yakhiwe kumhlaba oziihekthare eziyi-75 000 ongenabungozi besifo secesina (imalariya), kumbindi weMadikwe Game Reserve, le ndawo inamagumbi okulala ali-16 kunye nendawo yokutyela eyakhelwe ngaphandle kwisiqithi somlambo.
Igumbi ngalinye lokulala lihonjiswe ngokwahlukileyo kwaye lineveranda yalo eyodwa eyenziwe ngamaplanga.
IMadikwe River Lodge iphisa ngeentsuku ezintathu zokulala, izidlo ezithathu kunye nemidlalo yolonwabo emibini (ukuhamba nibuka izilwanyana okanye nilandela imizila yazo) ngemini.
Lingakuphosi eli thuba lokonwabela ubuhle bendalo, ubone iintaka ezinemibala-bala nezilwanyana zasendle.

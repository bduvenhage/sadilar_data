Zifakwe
II-JAMBOS zakwaBakers zizimuncu-muncu ezilungel' usapho. Zenziwe ngeebhiskiti ezikrum-krum ezifakwe ijam yaye zineencasa eziluncuthu ezintathu: eyeraspberry, eye-orenji neyeblackcurrant. Iipakethe ezincinci eziyi-33g zikulungel' ukutyiwa nanini na naphi na okanye ukuphathwa esikolweni nasemsebenzini, ukanti zona iipakethe eziyi-125g zilungel' usapho luphela. Iipakethe ezincinci ziyi-R2 inye, ezi zinkulu ziyi-R2.50, kuxhomekeka kwivenkile othenge kuyo. Yonwabel' incasa yeziqhamo zokwenene nomlingo wendoda yakwaBakers![x]
Uncuthu lweebhiskiti zakwaBakers iiJambos zinencasa yejam yeziqhamo yaye ziyafikeleleka!

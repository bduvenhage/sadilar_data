LUNGISA isinxibo sakho neenyawo zakho zikhululeke ngoFroggie. Nokuba unxibele ukuyaphi, ngayiphi indlela ethandwayo kweli xesha lonyaka, iFroggie yimfuneko kuwo onke amanenekazi.
Izihlangu zakwaFroggie zisemgangathweni wehlabathi kwaye zenziwa ngocoselelo ngesandla kusetyenziswa ufele lwexabiso.
Zenziwa kweli loMzantsi Afrika ngentsebenziswano nogqirha ophambili weenyawo, uFroggie unoluhlu lwezihlangu olulungele wonke ubani...
Ukuze ukhululeke jonga izihlangu ezineFlex Technology, evumela isihlangu ukuba silandele intshukumo yonyawo lwakho. Lukho uluhlu lweenyawo ezibanzi iFroggie Plus range, kanti abo bafuna ukujuxuza ubusuku bonke uFroggie unezihlangu, zofele ezithambileyo ezenzelwe kanye oko...

UKHUPHISWANO
U-AVON yenye yeenkampani ezithengisa izinto zokuthambisa eziphumeleleyo elizweni, kwaye ikuphathele okukodwa kule nyanga kaJulayi.
Indumasi kamabonakude uLesego Motsepe kutshanje wenziwe indwalutho esisithethi esitsha sakwa-Avon eMzantsi Afrika. Abakwa-Avon bazisa ngesiqholo sabo esitsha, i-Imari Seduction kule nyanga kaJulayi 2008.
"I-Imari Seduction sisiqholo esohlukileyo esinevumba elimnandi leziqhamo. Iyalunga xa uyifake emini, kanti ke nangokuhlwa ilunga ngakumbi kuba indenza ndizive ndinomtsalane. Ibhotile yayo iyabukeka, inombala opepile okhazimlayo oyenza ibukeke kwaye ifaneleke," utsho uLesego.
Ukuze uzivele ngokwakho i-Imari Seduction, ufumanise nangoluhlu olupheleleyo lweemveliso ze-Avon okanye ukuba ungenza njani ukuze ube ngomnye weqela labathengisi abaphumeleleyo bakwa-Avon, thetha nelungu lakwa-Avon elikufutshane kuwe, tsalela umnxeba abakwa-Avon ku-0860-10-23-45, okanye undwendwele iWebsite yabo ethi: www.avon.co.za

Ngamangenelo akhutshwe kwimagazini athunyelwa ngeposi kuphela aya kwamkelwa. 5. Baya kuphulukana namabhaso abantu abangawalandanga kwade kwaphela iinyanga ezi-6.

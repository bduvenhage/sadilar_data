Inkcazelo
Xa umenzi wesicelo engasayini isicelo ngokwakhe, ileta egunyazisa umntu osayinayo njengearhente yokuthi asayine egameni lomenzi wesicelo, kufuneka ikhaphe isicelo.
Xa umenzi wesicelo engene kwisivumelwano nomthumeli wangaphandle wesilwanyana okanye into engeniswa elizweni phantsi kwemiqathango yokuthi izabelo, iintlawuliso okanye iziyekelelo ziya kuhlawulwa ngaphezu kwexabiso lentengiso, kufuneka isicelo sikhatshwe yileta evela kuMlawuli Jikelele we-Department of Trade and Industry (ISebe lezoRhwebo noShishino) yokuqinisekisa ukuba iSebe lisamkele isivumelwano esinjalo.

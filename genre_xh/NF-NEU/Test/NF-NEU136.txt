BONA XHOSA November 2008
ILA Palms Spa eseFourways eGauteng yakhiwe kumhlaba oziihekthare ezili-12 yaye amabhadi neetsiba-bhokhwe ziyazihambela kumabala ayo. Inika iinkonzo ezahlukeneyo zempilo nezobuhle ukanti ikwanendawo yokuhlala abantu abafikelela kwi-15.
Iingcali zobuhle zaseLa Palms Day Spa zisebenzisa izithambiso ezikhethekileyo ze-Eva Garden ezenziwa yingcali yolungiso-buso yase-Italy, uPaulo Guatelli.
Ngenela olu khuphiswano namhlanje ukuze uzuze imini yokuya kuphululwa umzimba okanye uqhagamshelane neLa Palms ukuze uzifumanele icham leHalf-day Relaxation Package eliyi-R425 kumntu ngamnye. Kungoku nje babhalisa nabantu abafuna ukwenzela khona amatheko okuphela konyaka. Bafowunele kule nombolo: 011-460-1383; Email: lapalmsspa@polka.co.za; Website: www.lapalmsspa.co.za
Ongakuzuza
Ukuya kulungiswa umzimba okuxabisa i-R7 500 umntu, ukulungiswa umzimba iintsuku ezintathu okuxabisa i-R1 350 umntu okanye isethi yezithambiso nezinto zokulungis' ubuso ze-Eva Garden ezixabisa i-R1 350.
Ulungiso-mzimba olupheleleyo; ilantshi; ukuphululwa kwentloko nomzimba; ukucetyiswa ngembonakalo; ulungiso-buso neenwele; nokufotwa emini.
Ulungiso-mzimba lwemini luquka isidlo sakusasa esilula; ukucocwa komzimba, ukuphululwa entloko; ilantshi; ulungiso-buso; ukulolwa iinyawo nokulungiswa iinzipho.

inqaku lentengiso
NANGONA ubisi lungabalulekanga kangako ebantwaneni abasele bethe dlundlu njengakwiintsana, ludlala indima enkulu ekukhuleni kwabo kwaye iingcali zempilo zicebisa ukuba umntwana asele ubisi kabini okanye kathathu (400-600ml) ngemini.
I-Purity ivelise iPurity Growing-up Milk yabantwana abanonyaka ukusa kwabaneminyaka emi-3 olunesondlo kunobisi lwenkomo. Isondlo esongeziweyo sifumaneka kwiivithamini, iiminerali neefatty acids ezongeziweyo kwaye ziqinisekisa ukuba xa umntwana elusele kabini ubisi ngemini ufumana ama-50 ekhulwini eprotein, icalcium, ivitamins A, C, E, B2, B12 nepantothenic acid.
IPurity iphinde yahlangabezana nengxaki yesifo esixhaphakileyo sokuba buthathaka kwegazi ngokuthi yongeze uvitamin C onceda ukugcina i-ayoni. Iifatty acids eziyimfuneko ezongeziweyo zincedisa ukukhula kwengqondo nokubona lo gama icalcium ikhulisa amathambo namazinyo omeleleyo.[x]

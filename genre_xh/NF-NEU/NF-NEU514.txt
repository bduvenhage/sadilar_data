Inkcazelo
Zonke iimali ezifakwa kwiikhawuntari zasePosini azihlawulelwa.
Qaphela: Abantwana abangekabinalungelo lokuzenzela izigqibo ngokusemthethweni abanqwenela ukuvula ii-akhawunti mabakhatshwe ngabalondolozi babo basemthethweni, okanye ngumzali okanye abazali.
Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Ukuze uvule i-akhawunti, izinto nje ekufuneka uzenze zezi zilandelayo:
Yiya ePosini ekufutshane eMzantsi Afrika kwaye ucele umbali-mali akunike ifom yesicelo.
Abantwana abangekabinalungelo lokuzenzela izigqibo ngokusemthethweni abanqwenela ukuvula ii-akhawunti mabeze nabalondolozi babo basemthethweni, okanye nomzali okanye abazali.
Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo
Isicelo sithabatha imizuzu eli 10 ukuya kwengama 20, kuxhomekeke ukuba loo bhrentshi yeposi ixakeke kangakanani na.



Ukutshintsha imali yenkxaso yokunakekela omnye umntu  




Inkcazelo

Ungacela ukuba imali ehlawulelwa inkxaso yokunakekela omnye umntu  itshintshwe, ngenxa yokuba ayoneli okanye ngenxa yokuba uyoyisakala ukukhupha loo mali yesapoti.

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwiSebe lezoBulungisa noPhuhliso loMgaqo-siseko


Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Ukubangaba ungulo mntu ufumana isapoti:
Yenza isicelo kwinkundla kamantyi ekwisithili apho lo mntu uhlawula isapoti ahlala khona.
Gcwalisa ifom yesicelo efanelekileyo uze uyihambise kwigosa lesapoti, ikunye nesitetimenti sengeniso-mali kunye nenkcitho.

Ukubangaba ungulo mntu uhlawula isapoti kodwa iyakoyisa le mali uyikhuphayo:
Yenza isicelo sokulungiswa komyalelo kwi-ofisi kamantyi ekwisithili ekuhlala kuso lo mntu umsapotayo.
Gcwalisa ifom ebandakanyekayo kwaye uyihambise kwigosa lesapoti.

Hambisa isitetimenti esipheleleyo sengeniso-mali kunye nenkcitho, ndawonye nengxelo echaza izizathu zesicelo, kwigosa lesapoti, akukhathaliseki nokuba ungumzuzi okanye ungumhlawuli wemali yesapoti. Kuyakuthi ke kulandelwe inkqubo efanayo naxa kufakwa isibango sesapoti. 

Imithetho esetyenziswayo

Maintenance Act, 1998  and related Regulations UMthetho weNkxaso yokuNakekela omnye umntu, kunye neMigqaliselo eyayamene nawo

Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo

Uyakuphathwa ngokulinganayo, ngokunobuntu, nangesidima nentlonipho.
Isicelo sakho siyakuphathwa ngokukhawuleza nangobunono kangangoko kunokwenzeka
Faka nasiphina isikhalazo onganaso kuMphandi weNkxaso yokuNakekela omnye umntu , kwiGosa leNkxaso yokuNakekela omnye umntu, kuManejala weNkundla, kwiNtloko yeNgingqi kwezoBulungisa noPhuhliso loMgaqo-siseko okanye kwiZiko leNkonzo eGqwesileyo; kwiProjekthi yeNkxaso yokuNakekela omnye umntu yeSebe leLizwe lezoBulungisa noPhuhliso loMgaqo-siseko; okanye kwiCandelo loBuqondisi beMibandela ye-Gender.

Iindleko

Ngaphandle kokuba igosa elihambisa imiyalelo yenkundla  licela kuqale kukhutshwe imali, ukuze likwazi ukukhupha umyalelo wokuhlawula isapoti, iindleko ziyakubancinane okanye zingabikho konke konke.

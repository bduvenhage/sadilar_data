



1.	Yintoni injongo yokulungelelanisa iindibano zovakaliso-zimvo zoluntu ezingokunikezelwa kweenkonzo eluntwini?



Kutshanje iPalamente ithe yabamba umjikelo wokuqala weendibano zovakaliso-zimvo zoluntu ePalamente, ingaba zibe yintoni iziphumo zezi ndibano zovakaliso-zimvo?

Ngokwezindululo ezibhaliweyo ezingenisiweyo ngalo mbandela, ingaba yintoni ekhokelele kuqhankqalazo malunga nokunikezelwa kweenkonzo eluntwini?

Zeziphi iinkqubo ezizakuthi zilandelwe emva kwezi ndibano zovakaliso-zimvo zoluntu?

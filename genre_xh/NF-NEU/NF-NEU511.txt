Inkcazelo
Olu ncedo lulungiselelwe abantu abavelisa iimoto, abenza iimoto,abathenga bathengise iimoto. Inanai lokurhweba ngeemoto linika imvume kwababantu babaliweyo yokuba baqhube endleleni kawonke-wonke iimoto ezingabhaliswanga.
abavelisi bemoto, bazise bathengise batshintshe okanye balungise iimoto abathuthi bemoto bahambise iimoto abarhwebi ngemoto ukuze bakwazi ukuhambisa iimoto nabathenga iimoto kwamanye amazwe ukuze bakwazi ukuzihambisa.

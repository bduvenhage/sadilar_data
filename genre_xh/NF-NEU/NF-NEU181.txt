OKUMNANDI
Funda
ukupheka
noNtwenhle Mpanza
Kwenze kube lula ukonwabisa usapho lwakho nabahlobo xa befikile. Baphe amagwinya amnandi
IVETKOEK
* 4 iikomityi (1 000ml) zecake flour
* 1 itispuni (5ml) yetyuwa
* 2 amacephe (30ml) eswekile
* 1 x 10g ipakethe yedry yeast
* 1 1/2 ikomityi (375ml) yamanzi ashushu
* 3 iikomityi (750ml) ze-oyile
1. Sefela izithako ezomileyo esityeni. Galela esithebeni seplanga wenze umngxuma phakathi.
2. Galela amanzi kuhle emngxunyeni, uxove ngezandla de intlama idibane. Phindisela intlama esityeni, uyogqume ngefadukhwe, yiyeke yonyuke imizuzu engama- 45. (Ubungakanani bayo bumel' ukuphindeka kabini.)
3. Fudumeza i-oyile epanini enzulu kubushushu obuphantsi. (Xa ijika ibe
mdaka ibonisa ukuba sele ilungile.) Sebenzisa icephe nezandla, wenze intlama ibeziibhola ezincinci. Zifake e-oyileni kuhle. Wapheke de abebugolide. Waphake eshushu okanye ebanda.
Iingcebiso
Izithako ezomileyo zisefe ndawonye ukuze umxube ungene umoya.
Qiniseka ukuba amafutha ashushu, kodwa amagwinya uwapheke kubushushu obuphantsi.
Amagwinya avuthiweyo ungawasika ufake inkuku okanye ituna mayonnaise, imifuno, ikhari yangezolo, okanye nantoni na othanda ukuyitya. Ungawaqaba nejam.[x]

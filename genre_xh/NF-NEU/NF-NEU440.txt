

IKomiti eZimeleyo ngoMdibaniso yezobuNtlola imema izicelo zomsebenzi kwisikhundla somHloli Jikelele weeNkonzo zobuNtlola. Izicelo kufuneka zingqamane neCandelo le-7 loMthetho woLawulo lweeNkonzo zobuNtlola wama-40 we–1994. Thumela i-CV eneenkcukacha ezithe vetshe kunye nezizathu ezenza ukuba uqeshwe, uzithumele  kwiKomiti ungadlulanga umhla wesibini kweyeNkanga we-2009.

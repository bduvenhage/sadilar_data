Inkcazelo

Imvume yokuthumela ngaphandle kwelizwe evela ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement  iyafuneka phambi kokuthumela ngaphandle kwelizwe izilwanyana okanye izinto zofuzo ezifana nee-embryo, ii-ova okanye incindi yobudoda  ukuphuma eMzantsi Afrika. Kufuneka umbutho wabazalisi ofanelekileyo okanye igunyabantu elibhalisayo likhuthaze ukuthumela ngaphandle kwelizwe.

Kufuneka isicelo sinikwe uMbhalisi kwithuba leentsuku ezingama-30 phambi kokuthumela ngaphandle kwelizwe.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khupha ifomu yesicelo kwi-inthanethi uze uyizalise ngokupheleleyo. Ifomu kananjalo ingacelwa ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement .
Izicelo ezingazaliswanga ngokupheleleyo ziya kuphindiswa kumenzi wesicelo.
Qhoboshela izintlu ezibonisa iinkcukacha zabanikeli bee-embryo, ii-ova okanye incindi yobudoda okanye izilwanyana eziya kungeniswa elizweni njengakwicandelo C lefomu yesicelo.

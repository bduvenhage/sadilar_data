Inkcazelo
Le nkonzo ilungiselelwe umntu welizwe langaphandle onqwenela ukwenza isicelo sobume bokuhlala isigxina ngenxa yokuba enikwe ingqesho esisigxina eMzantsi Afrika. Esi sicelo kufuneka sibe ngaphakathi kweziqingqo zangonyaka ezibekwa kumaxesha ngamaxesha.
Izicelo zingenziwa kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya kwiphondo ojonge ukusebenza kulo okanye kwindlu yozakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiziko elimele uMzantsi Afrika ukubangaba wenza isicelo ukwilizwe langaphandle.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Umntu ozakubangumqashi wakho kufuneka athi thaca ubungqina bezi zinto zilandelayo kwiSebe lezeKhaya:
o bokuba isikhundla osinikwayo sikho o bokuba isikhundla kunye nenkcazelo yomsebenzi ozakuwenza sasikhutshiwe kwisibhengezo sokwazisa (advertised) ngendlela ebekiweyo o bokuba zange kubekho bemi baseMzantsi Afrika okanye abahlali abasisigxina abaneekhwalifikheyshini ezifanelekileyo ukuze bangene kweso sikhundla.
Department of Labour (ISebe leZabasebenzi), kufuneka liqinisekise ukuba iimeko nemiqathango yokunikwa le ngqesho ayikho ngaphantsi kwaleyo ikhoyo kwicandelo lemarike engqameneyo yabemi nabahlali. Ngokwenza njalo, iSebe leZabasebenzi lifuneka ukuba lithathele ingqalelo izivumelwano zeengxoxo ezihlanganelweyo kunye neminye imigangatho ebandakanyekayo.

UBUSAZI NA... ukuba AMASI AYINXALENYE YEEMVELISO ZOBISI?
Oko kuthetha ukuba emasini ufumana konke ukulunga kweemveliso zobisi:
Kufuneka utye iimveliso zobisi ubomi bakho bonke, ukwakha amathambo omeleleyo nokuwagcina omelele!
Ikhalsiyam yiminerali ekwiimveliso zobisi yaye iyanceda ukugcina amathambo ephilile yaye omelele.
Iimveliso zobisi zinazo neeprotini esizifunayo ukuze sikhule kakuhle.
Zikunika neevithamini neeminerali ezibalulekileyo ukuze ubesempilweni.
Makabekho amasi ezidlweni zakho yaye uzibandakanye nenkqubo yethu yokutya ekuthiwa yi-3-A-DAY™ Dairy!
YINTONI I-3-A-DAY™ dairy?
Ukukunceda uqinisekise ukuba uyifumana ngokwaneleyo ikhalsiyam sikwenzele inkqubo elula ye-3-A-DAY™ Dairy! Omawukwenze kukuqinisekisa ukuba utya iimveliso ezintathu zobisi ngemini. Kulula ukuyilandela kuba zininzi iimveliso zobisi ukususela elubisini, amasi, iyogathi netshizi!
UNGAZILIBALI IIMVELISO ZOBISI!

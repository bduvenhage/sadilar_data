Inkcazelo
Ilayisensi yomfundi-kuqhuba yilayisensi yokubambisa eqinisekisa ukuba unolwazi olusisiseko lokuqhuba isithuthi. Iikhowudi zelenasi zahlulwe zangamacandelo amathathu, kwaye icandelo ngalinye linemimiselo yeminyaka yobudala.
Ingqokelela yomthetho yokuqala (Code 1): Isithuthuthu nokuba ngowuphi umhlobo
Ingqokelela yomthetho yesibini (Code 2): Iimoto
Emotweni umceli kufuneka abe neminyaka elishumi elinesibhozo
Ingqokelela yomthetho yesithathu (Code 3): Imoto enkulu nokuba yeyiphi okanye umxube wazo, umceli kufuneka abe neminyaka elishumi elinesibhozo.
Umceli akazi kuvavanywa ngosuku olubekiwe kwaye nexesha lokulindela uvavanyo lwahlukile ngokomasipala.
Iphepha-mvume lokufunda ukuqhuba liphelelwa lixesha emva kwenyanga ezilishumi elinesibhozo (18 months) futhi angeke longezelelwe elixeha.

Xa usenza isicelo sephepha-mvume lokufunda ukuqhuba gcwalisa ifomu (LL1) endaweni yovavanyo ekufuphi nawe.

Kuza kufuneka wenze uvavanyo lwamehlo endaweni yovavanyo.

Inkcazelo
Le nkonzo inika inkcazelo malunga nendlela yokubhalisela ukubuyiselwa imali yi-South African Revenue Service. Le mali ibuyiswayo sisaphulelo kwintlawulo yokungenisa impahla nezinto ehlawuleka xa ungenisa impahla nezinto ezithile kweli lizwe.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Umenzi-sicelo, hayi umntu wesithathu, kufuneka ibenguye ogcwalise ifom yesicelo (DA 185), kungenjalo isicelo asiyi kuqwalaselwa.
o igama lokurhweba leshishini lakho o igama nenombolo yesitalato seshishini elibuyiselwayo o igama kunye nenombolo yomgangatho wesakhiwo ekuqhutyelwa kuso eli shishini o ummandla wedolophu elimi kuwo ishishini o isixeko okanye idolophu elimi kuyo ishishini o ikhowudi yesitalato sommandla wedolophu elimi kuwo ishishini.
Icandelo elithi Authority to apply (Igunya lokwenza isicelo), maligcwaliswe ngumenzi-sicelo (UMnikazi-shishini oZimele Yedwa, UMqondisi, Oogxa beNtsebenziswano, iLungu, iLungu eliPhathiswe iQumrhu ngeNtembeko okanye umntu ophethe naliphina elinye iqumrhu) xa usenza isicelo sokuba ngumsebenzisi wokubuyiswa kwemali.
Igama lomenzi-sicelo okanye igama lobhaliso lweshishini, hayi igama lorhwebo, kufuneka liboniswe kweli candelo.
Apho kubonakala khona inkwenkwezana ekuthiwa yi-asterisk (*), susa nayiphina efaneleke njalo.
o inkcazelo yempahla nezinto eziyakugcinwa kwishishini lokubuyiselwa imali o izinga lemirhumo yentlawulo okanye imiba yempahla nezinto ezingeniswayo eziyakugcinwa kwishishini lokubuyiselwa imali o imiba yokubuyiselwa imali phantsi kwamanani oludwe lwenkqubo yesi 3, 4, 5 kunye neyesi 6
o inkcazelo yemveliso engeniswa kweli lizwe okanye impahla nezinto ezingeniswa kweli lizwe.
Kwicandelo le Declaration (Isibhengezo), kufuneka ufake:
o oonobumba bamagama okuqala kunye nefani yomntu owenza isicelo sokunikwa ilayisensi o isikhundla somntu owenza isicelo, umzekelo, UMqondisi, uManejala, uMnikazi o lo mntu wenza isicelo kufuneka atyikitye (asayine) ifom yesicelo o umhla esisayinwe ngawo esi sicelo o indawo esisayinelwe kuyo esi sicelo.

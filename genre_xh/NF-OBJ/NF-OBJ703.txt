Ulwazi Kubarhwebi Bamanye Amazwe




INKCAZELO:

Isebe Lezentengiso Noshishino libonelela ngolwazi kubathengisi bamanye amazwe ngolawulo lwentengiso yamazwe ngamazwe, kuquka:
* ukulungiswa kweerhafu nezaphulelo
* ukungalahlwa nokulinganiswa
* ulawulo lokurhwebela ngaphakathi kwilizwe nangaphandle.

Inkcazelo

Olu ncedo lulungiselelwe abanini moto,abanemoto inkcukacha zazo ezitshintshileyo. Olu tshintsho luquka injini yemoto,okanye inani elichaza imoto .Kunyanzelekile wazise iCandelo lezoThutho xa kwenziwe olutshintsho.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Yiya e ofisini lamagosa endlela ekufutshane
Yiza nala maxwebhu alandelayo:

Isatifiketi okanye ingxelo efungelweyo yaMapolisa aseMzantsi Afrika eqinisekisa ukubasemthethweni kwemoto.
Isatifiketi sokubhaliswa kwemoto.

UKUTHENGA & NOKUBA NEKHAYA ELILELAKHO

Cofa kwinkonzo leyo xa ufuna iinkcukacha ezipheleleyo.


Amalungelo Obunini: Ubungqina Bokuba Ngumnini-mhlaba
Xa uthenga umhlaba, kufuneka ufumane ubungqina boko ngokuthi loo mhlaba ubhaliswe egameni lakho. Eli lungelo lobunini ke libubungqina bokuba ngokwenene unguye umnini walo mhlaba. Uya kudinga uncedo lwegqwetha kwimicimbi yempahla nemihlaba ukuze likuncedise kwinkqubo yokudluliselwa kwalo mhlaba egameni lakho. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

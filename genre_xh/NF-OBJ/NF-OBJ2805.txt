Inkcazelo

Iqumrhu elingavulelekanga ekuthiwa yi-close corporation  lelona qumrhu loshishino lilula kwaye lithambileyo kunenkampani. Liwafanele amashishini amancinane. Iimfuneko zokuphatha nokulawula ii-CCs zinobuceducedu bogunyaziso obungcono kunobo benkampani.

Igama elipoposhwayo le-CC kufuneka lihlolwe phambi kokuba livunywe okanye laliwe. I-Ofisi yoBhaliso lwaMashishini angaVulelekanga  iya kungqina ukuvunywa okanye ukwaliwa ngencwadi, ethunyelwe nge e-mail okanye ngeposi, ibonise ukuba igama libekiwe okanye laliwe.

Igama elivunyiweyo nelibekiweyo lisebenziseka iinyanga ezimbini.

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi , yiya kwi Companies and Intellectual Property Registration Office.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Zalisa ifom yesicelo ekuthiwa yi-CK7 kunye ne-CM5.
Hambisa ifom yesicelo kwi-ofisi ye-CIPRO.

Kuya kufuniswa ukuze kuqondwe ukuba akukho magama angafanelekanga, athelekiswa namanye, angafunekiyo okanye akhubekisayo na.

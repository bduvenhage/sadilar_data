Inkcazelo

nkonzo iqonda ukuba ingaba umenzi-sicelo ungummi waseMzantsi Afrika nyhani na phambi kokuba kukhutshwe iNcwadi yeSazisi. Isebenza kubantu ababengabemi baseMzantsi Afrika phambi kokuba balifulathele eli lizwe kwaye abajonge ukubuyela kubumi baseMzantsi Afrika. Izicelo zingenziwa kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya okanye kwindlu yozakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiziko elimele uMzantsi Afrika ukubangaba wenza isicelo ukwilizwe langaphandle.

Gcwalisa incwadi ethi 'To whom it may concern'.

Iinkcukacha zobuqu zomenzi-sicelo ziyahlolwa kwiNcwadi yeLizwe yoBhaliso lweNani neeNkcukacha zaBantu.

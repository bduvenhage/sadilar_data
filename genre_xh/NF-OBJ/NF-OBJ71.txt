Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Nceda ubhekise kwisikhokelo sesicelo ukuze sikuncede ngenkqubo yelayisensi.
o khangela ibhokisi yokuhlola yomthumeli wempahla nezinto ezithile kumazwe angaphandle, phantsi kobhaliso.
Igama lomenzi-sicelo okanye igama lobhaliso lweshishini, hayi igama lorhwebo, kufuneka liboniswe kweli candelo.
Apho kubonakala khona inkwenkwezana ekuthiwa yi-asterisk (*), susa nawuphina umba ofaneleke njalo.
Kwicandelo le Declaration (Isibhengezo), faka oku kulandelayo:
o oonobumba bamagama okuqala kunye nefani yomntu owenza isicelo sokunikwa ilayisensi o isikhundla somntu owenza isicelo, umzekelo, uMqondisi, uManejala, uMnikazi o umntu owenza isicelo kufuneka atyikitye (asayine) ifom yesicelo o umhla esisayinwe ngawo esi sicelo o indawo esisayinelwe kuyo esi sicelo.

Inkcazelo

UMthetho wokuLawula iMipu, ekuthiwa yi-Firearms Control Act, 2000, ujonge ukuqinisekisa ukuba umntu unolwazi kunye nezakhono zokubanompu.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Kufuneka ugqibe uvavanyo oluqingqiweyo ngempumelelo ukuze ungqine ulwazi lwakho loMthetho kumniki-nkonzo zoqeqesho onoqinisekiso lokuvunywa nokwamkelwa.

Inkcazelo

Abantu baseMzantsi Afrika abaneencwadi zezazisi ezineempazamo bangenza izicelo zokuzilungisa kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya. Izicelo zingenziwa nakwiNdlu yoZakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiZiko lobuMeli beli lizwe ukubangaba isicelo senziwa kwilizwe langaphandle.

Iimpazamo ezidla ngokufunyanwa kwiincwadi zezazisi, kwaye umenzi-sicelo anganqwenela ukuzilungisisa, zibandakanya ezi zilandelayo:

igama elingachanekanga kunye nefani okanye ifani engachanekanga
ifoto engeyiyo
i-adresi engachanekanga
umhla wokuzalwa ongachanekanga
isini  esingesiso.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa iifom zesicelo ekuthiwa zii BI-9, BI-526 kunye ne BI-309 kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya. Yenza isicelo kwiNdlu yoZakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiZiko lobuMeli beli lizwe ukubangaba ukwilizwe langaphandle.
Khupha ubungqina benkcazelo echanekileyo, umzekelo, isatifiketi sokuzalwa, isatifiketi sokuphehlelelwa, incwadi yesikolo okanye isatifiketi sokubeleka.
Hambisa nesicelo sakho iifoto ezimbini ezilingana kwi-ID.

Inkcazelo
Intlanganiso yoxolelwaniso yinkqubo apho umkomishina (okanye igqiza labaxoxi, kwimeko yebhunga okanye i-arhente yeengxoxo zokwenza izigqibo ezineenzuzo) ahlangana nemihlambi eyalanayo kwimpikiswano ukuze akhangele iindlela zokusombulula le mpikiswano ngesivumelwano.
Kwintlanganiso yoxolelwaniso umqeshi angaziyela ngokwakhe okanye angamelwa ngumqondisi okanye ngomnye umqeshwa okanye ngumbutho wabaqeshi. Umqeshwa angamelwa ngumngcini-sihlalo okanye ligosa lombutho obhalisiweyo womqeshwa.
Ukubangaba umenzi-sicelo akaphumelelanga ukuya kwintlanganiso yoxolelwaniso ngexesha elibekiweyo, lo mbandela uya kuchithwa. Ukubangaba umenzi-sicelo unqwenela ukuqhubeka, kufuneka kwenziwe isicelo sokurhoxisa oko kuchithwa.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Thumela ikopi yale fom kulo uphikisana naye.
Nika ubungqina bokuba ikopi yale fom isiwe kulo uphikisana naye, ngokufaka ezi zinto zilandelayo kwisicelo sakho:
o ikopi yesiliphu serejista esiphuma ePosini o ikopi yerisiti esayiniweyo ukubangaba ikopi isiwe ngesandla o ingxelo esayiniweyo yalo mntu uhambise ikopi yefom, engqina ukusiwa kwefom o ikopi yesiliphu esingqinayo sefeksi okanye nabuphi na obunye ubungqina obanelisayo benkonzo.

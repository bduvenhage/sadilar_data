Inkcazelo

Naliphina ishishini eliqhuba imisebenzi efanelekileyo kwaye elibhalisele iinjongo zerhafu yabaniki-nkonzo ekuthiwa yi-VAT, phantsi koMthetho weRhafu ekhutshwa ngabaniki-nkonzo, ekuthiwa yi-Value-Added Tax Act, 1991-nokuba kungokwesinyanzelo  okanye ngokuzithandela -lingenza isicelo sokubhalisela ukubuyiselwa imali yedizili, ngokugcwalisa ifom ekuthiwa yi-VAT 101D .

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Qhagamshelana neNkonzo yeNgeniso yaseMzantsi Afrika kwi-ofisi ekwingingqi yakho  ukuze ufumane ifom yokwenza isicelo. Okunye ongakwenzayo, kukuyikopa  ifom - Application for registration of diesel refund, VAT 101D kunxibelelwano lwekhompyutha uze uyizalise okanye bhalisa ngonxibelelwano lwekhompyutha.
Abaniki-nkonzo ababhalisiweyo kufuneka bafake inombolo yabo yobhaliso lwe-VAT. Abenzi-zicelo abangayibhaliselanga i-VAT kufuneka bagcwalise ifom ekuthiwa yi-VAT101 ukuze bafumane inombolo yobhaliso lwe-VAT, phambi kokuba benze isicelo sokubhaliswa kwiNkqubo yokuBuyisa iMali yeDizili .
Umhla woxanduva awunakuba phambi komhla we 4 Julayi 2001. Umhla woxanduva lobhaliso olutsha iyakuba ngulo mhla umenzi-sicelo angene ngawo kuxanduva lokufaneleka abhalisele i-VAT.
Imali ebuyiswayo yedizili iyakuhlawulwa kwi-akhawunti yebhanki echaziweyo ngoTshintshelo loMatshini weBhanki . Akukho zitshekhi ziyakukhutshwa. Iinkcukacha zebhanki ezinikwe kwifom ekuthiwa yi-VAT 101D ziyakuthabatha indawo yeenkcukacha zebhanki ezikwinkqubo ye-VAT, ukubangaba azifani.
Yazisa i-SARS ukubangaba iinkcukacha zebhanki ziguqukile.

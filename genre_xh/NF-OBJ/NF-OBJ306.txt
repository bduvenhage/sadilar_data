IZiko loPhuhliso lweMveliso yoMzantsi Afrika Ethengiselwa Amazwe Angaphandle




INKCAZELO:

IZiko leMveliso Ethengiselwa Amazwe Angaphandle lamiselwa kuMzantsi Kapa ukunyusa umgangatho wemveliso ephuma kule ngingqi. Imiswe kwi- Ziko Loshishino loMzantsi Kapa eGeorge, Ziko elo likhuphela imveliso kumazwe angaphandle linikezela abo bathengisela amazwe angaphandle abakhasayo nabasele bekhona ngengcebiso exabisekileyo kwakunye nolwazi lorhwebo.Ezona nkonzo zingundoqo kweli Ziko zezi:
* uqeqesho
* ulwazi
* ingcebiso
* imisebenzi yorhwebo (lwangaphakathi nolwangaphandle).

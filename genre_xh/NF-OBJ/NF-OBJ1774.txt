ICape Net




INJONGO:

Injongo ephambili yale projekthi kukuhlola uthungelwano nokulunguza amathuba akhoyo ngaphakathi kwimibutho yamacandelo oluntu eNtshona Koloni


ISIQALO:

1 uMeyi 2003


ISIPHELO:

30 uNovemba 2003


UMGANGATHO:

Liqoshelisiwe


INKCAZELO:


Le yiprojekthi yezibonelelo zomsebenzi kubuchwepheshe nehlola ukuba nokwamkelwa kothungelwano olufanayo nomzekelo we- Malaysian Multimedia Corridor model.

Inqanam lokuqala lale projekthi liquka ukwenziwa komlinganiselo wolwazi lwezihamba-ndlela, ukuquka iindawo ezikhoyo zokuhlola kwi-PGWC, SITA, TENET kunye neSixeko saseKapa.

Inqanaba lesibini liquka isiphumo sohlahlelo lwamashishini kwimilinganiselo yolwazi ngezihamba-ndlela kwakunye nokuphuhlisa ufundo kunye/okanye imiba ethile kwezoshishino.

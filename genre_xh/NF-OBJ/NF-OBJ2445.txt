Inkcazelo

Ipaspoti yexeshana elifutshane inikwa abantu baseMzantsi Afrika ekufuneka bathabathe uhambo olukhawulezileyo kwaye abangenako ukulinda ukukhutshwa kwepaspoti yesiqhelo. Ipaspoti yexeshana elifutshane isebenziseka iinyanga ezili 12. Xa usenza isicelo sepaspoti yexeshana elifutshane, kukwafuneka wenze nesicelo sepaspoti yesigxina.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa ifom yesicelo ekuthiwa yi BI-73 kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya eMzantsi Afrika.
Hambisa:

incwadi echaza ukuba kutheni udinga ipaspoti yexeshana elifutshane
incwadi yakho yesazisi okanye isatifiketi sokuzalwa
iifoto ezine ezilingana kwipaspot
incwadi ebhaliweyo nefungelweyo  ukubangaba ipaspoti yakho yaseMzantsi Afrika yalahleka okanye yabiwa.

Inkcazelo

Ukuhlelwa ngokukhethekileyo kwemoto kuvumeleka kuhlobo oluthile lwemoto elenzan umsebenzi othile elingavamisanga ukuhamba endleleni kawonke-wonke.

Inqwelo erhuqwayo okanye itrekta ikhululiwe ekubhataleni indleko zephepha-mvume ukuba umnini wazo ungumlimi ngokunyanisekileyouzisebenzisela ulimo kuphela.Kuyafana naxa imoto yakhiwe ngokutsha okanye itshintshiwe ukuze isetyenzselwe ukhuphiswano okanye imiboniso.

isatifiketi sokulungela ukuba sendleleni kwemoto.

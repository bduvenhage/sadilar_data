Inkcazelo
Ukuze uvumeleke, kufuneka ube ungumntu owayekade edlelwa indlala (oko kukuthi uNtsundu, uliKhaladi okanye uliNdiya)
ubeneminyaka yobudala eli 18 okanye ngaphezulu ube ujonge ukusebenzisa umhlaba ngeenjongo zezolimo kuphela ube ujonge ukubangumfama ngokupheleleyo ube awunasikhundla osibambileyo kumacandelo karhulumente uzimisele ukuthabatha inxaxheba kwinkqubo yoqeqesho emva kokuba ufumene umhlaba ubekwimeko yokuba ungenza igalelo ubeliqumrhu elakhiwe ngokwamacandelo ukubangaba nenza isicelo ningumbutho, kwaye ubene-akhawunti yebhanki.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Chaza umhlaba ofuna ukuwuthenga. Kungenjalo, mhlawumbi sele unawo umhlaba, ofana nomhlala onabanikazi abawudibaneleyo.
Nxulumana neSebe lezeMihlaba okanye igosa lendawo ohlala kuyo lesihlomelo sezolimo (agricultural extension) ukuze wenze isicelo segranti.
Hambisa le miqulu ilandelayo nesicelo sakho:
o indlela yokukhetha ukuthengisa ngexabiso ekuvunyelwene ngalo ukubangaba ungenela isivumelwano sengqeshiso esinendlela yokukhetha ukuthenga o uluhlu lwamalungu ekhaya okanye amalungu ombutho, ukubangaba umpoposho ngowombutho o ubungqina bokuba itayitile kulo mhlaba icacile, ayinazibango zomhlaba, ibhaliswe ngegama lomthengisi kwaye ixabiso ekuthetha-thethwane ngalo alikho ngaphezulu kwexabiso eliphakanyiswe yimarike o ubungqina bokuba le mali iseleyo ikhona (iimali ezizezo-menzi-sicelo okanye ikhontrakthi yemali-mboleko elungiselelayo)
o ingxelo yokuphonononga ixabiso

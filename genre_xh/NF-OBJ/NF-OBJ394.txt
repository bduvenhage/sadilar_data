Iveki Yamashishini Asakhasayo ka-2007




UMHLA:

16 - 18 uAgasti 2007


IBAKALA:

Umboniso


INDAWO:

eKapa, iNtshona Kapa


INKCAZELO:

Le Veki Yamashishini Asakhasayo ngumsitho waminyaka le oxhaswa ngezimali nguRhulumente Wephondo leNtshona Kapa kwakunye neSixeko saseKapa. Lo msitho ke uquka Iindibano Zenkxaso Kumashishini kwakunye nomboniso owenziwa simahla yaye ujolise ekuboneleleni amashishini asakhasayo ngamathuba okufaka izicelo zeenkxaso-mali, awokwakha ubudlelwane bentsebenziswano kwanawokuphuhlisa izakhono.

Le Veki Yamashishini Akhasayo ka-2007 iyakube ibanjelwe eCape Town International Convention Centre (eCTICC) ukususela ngomhla weshumi ukuya koweshumi elinesibini kwinyanga yeThupha ka-2007.
Iwebsite yale Veki Yamashishini Asakhasayo (www.smallbusinessweek.co.za) iqulethe iinkcukacha ngalo msitho, nkcukacha ezo ziqulethe nolwazi:
* ngezinye iindibano
* imiboniso
* nabaxhasi.



UQHAGAMSHELWANO:

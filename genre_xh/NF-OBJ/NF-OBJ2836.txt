Inkcazelo

Inani lobhaliso endleleni likhutshelwa abemi bamazwe angaphandle abangenayo incwadi yesazisi yaseMzantsi Afrika, ithathwa njengendlela eyamkelekileyo yenani lokwazisa elisetyenziswa kulo naluphina urhwebo endleleni.Inani lobhaliso edleleni liyafuneka xa ummi welizwe langaphandle efuna ukubhalisa imoto eMzantsi Afrika.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Yiya kwi ofisi yamagosa endlela ekufutshane.
Gcwalisa ifomu ANR,yeSicelo neSazisi ngeNani loBhaliso eNdleleni.
Yiza nala maxwebhu alandelayo:


ikopi yencwadi yokundwendwela eqinisekisiweyo okanye imvume yokuhlala yethutyana
incwadi yesazisi ekhutshwe ngokusemthethweni.

Inkcazelo
Ipaspoti yokukhenketha yincwadi efunekayo xa undwendwela kwelinye ilizwe okanye xa uphinda ungena eMzantsi Afrika. Ipaspoti yokukhenketha ikhutshelwa abemi baseMzantsi Afrika abaneminyaka yobudala eli 16 okanye nangaphezulu. Iyasebenza kundwendwelo lwawo wonke amazwe ngaphandle kokuba kugunyaziswe ngolunye uhlobo. Isebenza iminyaka eli 10 ukusukela ngomhla ekhutshwe ngawo.
Kufuneka ubenencwadi yesazisi (ID) okanye isatifiketi sokuzalwa ukuze wenze isicelo. Isicelo sepaspoti kunye nesencwadi yesazisi ungazenza ngexesha elinye ukubangaba awunako ukulinda.

Ukubhaliswa Kwabavelisi, Abathumeli-mpahla kweli Nabenzi beziThuthi




INKCAZELO:

Sonke isithuthi ezenziwe apha eMzantsi Afrika okanye eso senziwe kwelinye ilzwe ze sathunyelwa kweli kufuneka sibhaliswe ngaphambi kokuba sithunyelwe kwindawo yentengiso okanye sithengiswe.


IMIYALELO:

Ukuba ungumenzi weemoto okanye ungumthumeli weemoto kweli, kufuneka ufake iphetshana lesiceloelingu-MVR1-MIB kwi-ofisi ekubhaliswa kuyo izithuthi.Eli phetshana kufuneka lihambe kunye:

* nesazisi somnini wesithuthi (ukuba isithuthi eso sithengwe phantsi kwesivumelwano sentengiselwano)
* isazisi somnini wemoto
* imali engummiselo ehlawulwayo
* isiqinisekiso esikhutshwa ngumvelisi, umenzi zimoto okanye umthengisi ozizisa kweli:
  * inombolo yomzimba wemoto
  * inombolo yenjini
  * uhlobo lwemoto
  * igama lodidi lwayo
  * i-tare ngokweekilograms
  * ubukhulu benjini ngokweesentimitha zetyhubhiki
  * amandla apheleleyo enjini
  * owona mbala wayo
  * inombolo yedidi

* isiqinisekiso samapolisa esingqina ukuba akukho kwhiniba likhoyo malunga nemoto le
* isiqinisekiso sokuba iyilungele indlela.

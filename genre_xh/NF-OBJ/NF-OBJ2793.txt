Inkcazelo

Umntu onesiqinisekiso semvume yesilwanyana sokunikela ngencindi yobudoda, angenza isicelo ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement  ngenjongo yokuhlaziya ubhaliso.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khupha ifomu yesicelo kwi-inthanethi uze uyizalise ngokwahlukanisa oonombumba usebenzisa oonobumba abakhulu. Ifomu kananjalo ingacelwa ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement .
Kufuneka ugqirha wezilwanyana angqine ukuba isilwanyana samkelekile malunga:

nemo yempilo ngokubanzi
nokungabinazo izifo
nodidi lwencindi yobudoda.



Kufuneka izicelo zifike kuMbhalisi kungagqithanga iintsuku ezingama-30 phambi kokuba imvume iphelelwe. Xa isicelo sifika kwiintsuku ezingama-90 emva kokuphelelwa kwemvume, kufuneka kuhlawulwe intlawuliso eyongezelelweyo emiselweyo.
Yenza itsheki okanye iposoda ehlawuleka ku-: The Director-General: Agriculture.

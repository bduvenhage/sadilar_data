Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Le fom yesicelo kufuneka igcwaliswe ngumenzi-sicelo, hayi umntu wesithathu, kungenjalo isicelo asiyi kuqwalaselwa.
Nceda ubhekise kwisikhokelo sesicelo ukuze sikuncede ngenkqubo yelayisensi.
o igama lokurhweba leshishini lakho o indawo elimi kuyo elishishini (umzekelo: Plot 10)
o igama lesitalato kunye nenombolo yeshishini o ummandla wedolophu elimi kuwo ishishini o isixeko okanye idolophu elimi kuyo ishishini; kunye o nekhowudi yesitalato sommandla wedolophu elimi kuwo ishishini.
Icandelo elithi Authority to Apply (Igunya lokwenza isicelo) maligcwaliswe ngumnikazi-shishini ozimele yedwa, umqondisi, oogxa bentsebenziswano, ilungu, ilungu eliphathiswe iqumrhu ngentembeko, okanye umntu ophethe ishishini xa usenza isicelo sokubhalisela ukubangumnikazi-fektri ochongekileyo malunga nemirhumo yempahla engena elizweni kunye nerhafu yempahla nezinto kunye neelayisensi ezithile phantsi kwe-AGOA .
Igama lomenzi-sicelo okanye igama lobhaliso lweshishini, hayi igama lorhwebo, kufuneka liboniswe kweli candela.
o apho kubonakala khona inkwenkwezana ekuthiwa yi-asterisk (*), susa nayiphina efaneleke njalo.
Kwicandelo le Declaration (ISibhengezo), faka oku kulandelayo:
o oonobumba bamagama okuqala kunye nefani yomntu owenza isicelo sokunikwa ilayisensi o isikhundla somntu owenza isicelo, umzekelo, uMqondisi, uManejala, uMnikazi o lo mntu wenza isicelo kufuneka atyikitye (asayine) ifom yesicelo o umhla esisayinwe ngawo esi sicelo o indawo esisayinelwe kuyo esi sicelo.
Faka kwifom yesicelo iikopi eziluqobo lwazo okanye eziqinisekisiweyo zale miqulu ilandelayo:
o isatifiketi sobhaliso lweshishini njengoko sikhutshwe nguMphathi woBhaliso lweeNkampani okanye iNqununu yeNkundla ePhezulu kwimeko yeQumrhu lokuGcina ngeNtembeko.
o isindululo okanye imvume okanye elinye igunya elibandakanyekayo.
o iincwadi zezazisi xa umntu engummi waseMzantsi Afrika
Yomntu ongahlali kweli lizwe iqumrhu elingavulelekanga kunye nequmrhu lokugcina ngentembeko - onke amalungu, ogxa bentsebenziswano okanye amalungu aphathiswe iqumrhu ngentembeko inkampani - bonke abaqondisi, kubandakanywa umqondisi olawulayo kunye nomqondisi wezemali.

Inkcazelo

Iqumrhu elingavulelekanga ekuthiwa yi-close corporation  lisenokuba likhule ngokuvokothekileyo, kwaye ukuze liqhubeke ukwanda, abanikazi bezabelo bafuneka ukuba bafake imali eyongezelelweyo kwi-CC.

Okanye, kusenokuba i-CC ikhuphisana neenkampani ezinkulu kwaye ifuna ukubayinkampani ukuze yenze imisebenzi yayo izinziseke kwaye ikhuphisane ngamandla.

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwi Companies and Intellectual Property Registration Office.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Ukubangaba igama le-CC lizakutshintshwa, kufuneka kuzaliswe ifom ekuthiwa yi-CM5.
Yenza ikopi yefom ekuthiwa yi-CM1 ukuya kutsho kwi-CM44C
Faka isitetimenti esisuka kwigosa elimele iinkonzo zequmrhu elingavulelekanga.

Ukubangaba kukho iinguqu eziyakwenziwa, umzekelo ilungu elishiya isikhundla , ukutshintsha kwexesha ekuphela ngalo unyaka-mali okanye kuqashwa igosa elitsha elimele iinkonzo, kufanele kufakwe ifom ekuthiwa yi-CK2 kunye ne-CK2A phambi okanye emva kokuguqulwa kwequmrhu. Iifom zokulungisa iqumrhu elingavulelekanga zizaliswa kwi-online.

Akunyanzelekanga ukuba igosa elimele iinkonzo kunye nomphicothi weencwadi zemali kwiqumrhu elingavulelekanga, ibengumntu omnye.

Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo

Ukubangaba akukho miqulu efakwe ngokwahlukeneyo kwimiqulu yenkampani, kuya kuthabatha iintsuku ezimbini ukuya kwezintlanu ukuze kuguqulwe iqumrhu elingavulelekanga libeyinkampani. Ukubangaba imiqulu ye-CK yafakwa ngokwahlukileyo, inkqubo iya kuthabatha iiveki ezintathu ukuze igqitywe.

Ngubani Onoxanduva Lokubonelela Umntwana Ngeemfuno Zakhe Ezingundoqo?



Umthetho ufuna umntwana axhaswe okanye ondliwe:


1. ngabazali bakhe, nokuba batshatile, bayahlalisana, abahlali kunye, okanye bawuqhawula umtshato wabo, kuquka nabazaliabafumene umntwana ukuba abe lilungu elitsha lekhaya loo mntwana; kunye
2. nabazali babazali bakhe, ukuba abazali bomntwana babetshatene.
3. Olu xanduva lubekwe ngumthetho lubizwa ngokuba "luxanduva lokukhulisa" okanye "uxanduva lokuxhasa".

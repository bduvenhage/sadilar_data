Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Yenza isicelo kwindlu yozakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiziko elimele uMzantsi Afrika.
Hambisa incwadi esuka kwiSebe leMfundo okanye kwiziko loluntu lemfundo ephakamileyo eMzantsi Afrika:
o engqina ukuba eli ziko lilo eliququzelela okanye eliphethe le nkqubo o echaza imisebenzi yale nkqubo kunye nexesha eliyakuthatyathwa yile nkqubo o engqina ukuba eli ziko liyakuthabatha uxanduva olupheleleyo malunga nalo mfundi ngelixa ekwiRiphabhlikhi kwaye umfundi wamkelelwe ukuba abhaliswe.
Hambisa incwadi esuka kwiziko elithile lelizwe langaphandle:
o engqina iinkcukacha zomfundi, kubandakanywa ubhaliso lomfundi kwiziko lemfundo ekwinqanaba lesithathu kwilizwe langaphandle o engqina umhla eyakuqala ngawo le nkqubo eMzantsi Afrika.

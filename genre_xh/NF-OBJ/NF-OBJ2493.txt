Inkcazelo

Esi sicelo sithunyelwa kwi-South African Revenue Service  xa kufunwa isikhokelo sokuxhuzula irhafu malunga neentlawulo nje ezisecaleni ezisisixa sesambuku, eziphuma kubaqashi .

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Qinisekisa ukuba imibandela yakho yerhafu ime kakuhle phambi kokuba wenze isicelo sesikhokelo serhafu. I-SARS ayidli ngokusikhupha isikhokelo serhafu ukubangaba kukho iincwadi zokubuyisa zerhafu ongekazibuyisi okanye kukho iintlawulo ongekazenzi.
Khupha ezi zinto zilandelayo ukuze uncedise i-SARS ekuvumeni isicelo sakho sesikhokelo serhafu:

ifom yesicelo ekuthiwa yi-IRP3
i-ID yakho kunye nenombolo yakho yokwazisa yerhafu 
inombolo yomqashi wakho yokwazisa ye-PAYE.


Entla

Umthetho osetyenziswayo

Income Tax Act, 1962  

Entla

Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo

Sakube sivunyiwe isicelo yi-SARS, kuyakuthunyelwa ubungqina kwisithuba seentsuku ezimbalwa, kodwa sona isikhokelo esigunyazisiweyo serhafu singathabatha ukuyakutsho kwiiveki ezine.

Eyona ndlela ilula kakhulu yokwenza isicelo sesikhokelo kukusifaka nge-eFiling. Isicelo sakho sesikhokelo esenziwe ngale ndlela sidla ngokuvunywa kwisithuba seeyure ezingama 48.

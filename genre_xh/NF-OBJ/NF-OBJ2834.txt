Inkcazelo

ISebe leZolimo livavanya iisampuli ezigunyazisiweyo zembewu ukuze iqinisekise uhlambuluko, ukuntshula kunye nokuphila.

Ziindidi zembewu ebhengezwe ngokomthetho i-Plant Improvement Act, 1976, kuphela ezigunyaziselwa ukuthathelwa iisampuli nokuvavanywa ukuze kuqondwe umgangatho wazo. Iisampuli ezigunyazisiweyo ziyavavanywa ngeenzame zokuqinisekisa ukuba kuthengiswa kuphela imbewu ehambisana nemimiselo yomthetho i-Plant Improvement Act, 1976, kwaye ephawulwe ngokuchanekileyo nangokunyanisekileyo.

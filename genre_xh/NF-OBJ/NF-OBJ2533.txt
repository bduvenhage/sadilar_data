Inkcazelo

Umnikazi wemoto angalucima ubhaliso lwemoto xa ingasahambi emgaqweni kawonke–wonke okanye ikhutshiwe endleleni.ngokungenasiphelo.

Xa kuphela iminyaka emine imoto ingena phepha-mvume, amagosa endlela akhupha isitifiketi sokuyicima lo moto endleleni.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa ifom e ofisini lamagosa endlela.
Nikezela ngezi nkcukacha zilandelayo:

incwadi yesaziso eqinisekisiweyo
ikopi eqinisekisiweyo yobunini moto
isitifiketi sobhaliso semoto.


Entla

Umthetho osetyenziswayo



National Road Traffic Act, 1996

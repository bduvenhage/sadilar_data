Inkcazelo

Umntu ovelisa, akhe, aguqule okanye angenise iimoto zangaphandle eMzantsi Afrika ngenjongo zorhwebo, okanye ukuthengisa kufuneka abhalise KwiCandelo leZothutho.

Lisakufumana isicelo iCandelo lezoThutho lizakuthumela umhloli oza kuhlolisisa umceli ukuba uyilandele imigomo efanelekileyo.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Dibana neCandelo leZothutho kwiphondo lakho.
Yiza nalamaxwebhu alandelayo:

ikopi eqinisekisiweyo yencwadi yesazisi
ikopi eqinisekisiweyo yesatifiketi soshishino
incwadi yelungelo lokumela xa umele inkampani
inani lengqokelela lomthetho wekxaso ophuma kuNcedo laBabuti beRhafu eMzantsi Afrika, xa ungenisa impahla yangaphandle eMzantsi Afrika
isatifiketi esisusa ukuthandabuzeka kwemoto esiphuma kuNcedo laMapolisa ase Mzantsi Afrika.

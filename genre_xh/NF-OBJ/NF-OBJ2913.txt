Inkcazelo
Ilungelo lokukhusela umsebenzi othile owenzileyo (i-copyright), lilungelo lokukhusela umsebenzi othile umntu awenzileyo, ongaba ngumsebenzi obhaliweyo kunye nomsebenzi oluqobo lwemvelaphi yawo, iinkqubo zeekhpmpiyutha, imisebenzi yobugcisa, izinto nezixhobo zosasazo, umculo, imiboniso-bhanyabhanya (iifilimu) okanye iividiyo.
Eli gama lithi "Protect" ("khusela") lithetha ukunqanda abanye bangakwazi ukukopa okanye ukuvelisa ngokutsha umsebenzi okwafana naloo msebenzi uwenzileyo okanye loo nto uyenzileyo, kunye nokuwusebenzisa (ngaphandle kwemvume yakho) ukuze bazalisekise iinjongo zokuzizuzela imali. UMthetho waMalungelo okuKhusela iMisebenzi ethile eyenziweyo (Copyright Act, 1978) unika imiyalelo ye-copyright.
Ngokubanzi, umntu obhale, oshicilele, opapashe, obonise ngokomdlalo, oqingqe, ozobe, ofote okanye ofake kwiingxelo zokugcina, umsebenzi othile, ungumnikazi welungelo lokukhusela loo msebenzi (copyright), ngokuzenzekelayo. Nangona kunjalo, ngamanye amaxesha, xa umntu eyalelwe ukuba enze umsebenzi othile, kwaye ehlawulwe ukuze enze umsebenzi othile, ilungelo lokukhusela loo msebenzi iba lelomqashi.
Kwimisebenzi emininzi,(ngaphandle kwemiboniso-bhanyabhanya), akuyomfuneko ukwenza isicelo selungelo lokukhusela umsebenzi (i-copyright).
Ilungelo lokukhusela umsebenzi othile owenzileyo malunga nemiboniso-bhanyabhanya okanye iividiyo, ezenzelwe ukusetyenziselwa urhwebo, kufuneka lenzelwe isicelo ngokugunyazisiweyo, ngokwale miqulu ilandelayo, efumaneka ngaphandle kwentlawulo kwi-Ofisi yaMalungelo okuKhusela iMisebenzi ethile eyenziweyo (Copyright Office):

Inkcazelo

Ukubangaba ikomiti yeBhunga laseMzantsi Afrika leZifundo zoQeqesho lobuNzululwazi bezeNdalo, ekuthiwa yi-South African Council for Natural Scientific Professions , iyala ukukubhalisa okanye icima ubhaliso lwakho, ungafaka isibheno kwiBhunga ukhalazele eso sigqibo kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama 30, uze ke uhlawule imali eqingqiweyo.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Nxulumana ne-SACNASP kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama 30 ukusukela kumhla wokufumana isaziso sokwaliwa kwesicelo sakho kwaye uyakucetyiswa ngomawukwenze.
Kungenjalo, hambisa incwadi yesibheno kwiBhunga kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama 30.
Incwadi kufuneka ikhatshwe yimali yentlawulo eqingqiweyo eyakuqulungqwa liBhunga.

Entla

Umthetho osetyenziswayo



Academy of Science of South Africa Act, 2001  
Natural Scientific Professions Act, 2003 

Entla

Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo

Kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama 60 ukusukela kumhla wokufunyanwa kwesibheno sakho, iBhunga liyakwenza isigqibo ngesibheno kwaye likunike izizathu zesigqibo salo.
Ukubangaba isibheno sibhekise ekwaleni kwekomiti ukuvuselela ubhaliso lomntu obhalisiweyo, ubhaliso lwakho alunakucinywa kude kugqitywe ngesibheno.

Masenza umculo!


13 uJanuwari 2006
UMTHOMBO WEENDABA Icandelo loMphathiswa Wemicimbi Yenkcubeko, Ezemidlalo Nolonwabo (uRhulumente Wephondo leNtshona Kapa)







Masenza umculo!

ISebe Lemicimbi yeNkcubeko NeMidlalo sekumbovu ukuba lenze inkqubo yokumisa i-Ofisi ezimeleyo yeziBonelelo zeeMvumi, eza kuqwalasela imiba emayela namagcisa okwenza anobuncutshe.

Bonke abantu abakhoyo nabahlumayo kwimveliso yomculo bayamenywa ukuba banikeze ulwazi kwaneenkcukacha ukuze kudityaniswe uvimba wolwazi wephondo, oza kulawulwa yi-Ofisi yeziBonelelo zeeMvumi.

Abantu ababhaliswe kuvimba wolwazi baza kumenywa kwiNgqungquthela yeeMvumi zePhondo ngonyaka wama-2006, ukuze emva koko kwenziwe ubume benkonzo kunye nesicwangciso sokwenza se-Ofisi yeziBonelelo.

Ingaba uthabatha inxaxheba ku/kwi?

* mculo wase-Afrika (owezemvelo nowangoku/ijazz/iKlasikhi/oweekwayala/iHip Hop/owamaRasta/oweVangeli/iRap/iKwaito/ owesiNgqisho neBlues/umculo waseKapa wexesha eliphakathi (minstrel)
* joki zeDiskhi (DJ’s)/kubuNjineli beSandi/kubaculi baseSityudiyo/kumaGcisa
* baphandi bezoMculo/kootitshala boMculo/kubahlohli bezoMculo, kubaVelisi bezoMculo/kubaBhexeshi beekwayala/kubaQambi beengoma
* baXhasi/kwii-Arhente/kooManejala

Imibutho kunye neencutshe ezizodwa zomculo ezingena kolu luhlu nakwezi ntlobo ezikhankanywe ngentla kufuneka banikeze olu lwazi lulandelayo:

1. Igama elipheleleyo nefani
2. Isiza sobuncutshe esiphakathi kolu shishino
3. Iinkcukacha zoqhagamshelwano kuqukwa: iposi yedilesi, i-imeyile kunye neenombolo zeefowuni.
4. Iwonga leNgqesho phakathi kweli shishini: sisigxina okanye likhesela
5. Xela ukuba ingaba unonxibelelwano lwehlabathi na - cacisa
6. Ulwazi ngawe kuqukwa: isini kunye nenombolo yesazisi

Umhla weshumi elinesithathu, kwinyanga ka 20 Februwari kunyaka ka 2006.

Amaphepha okubhalisa ayafumaneka kwiziko lethu, inkcukacha malunga nomba wokubhalisa uyafumaneka kwinqila ezikututshane ezithile. Ngolwazi oluphangaleleyo ningasifowunela.

QAPHELA: Uvimba wolwazi uza kusetyenziselwa ukunika ulwazi olululo kwiincutshe zomculo nakwabanye abanomdla kushishino lwezomculo, ukuze kwakhiwe ushishino lwezomculo nokuqinisekisa ufikelelo olulinganayo kwizibonelelo zolu shishino. Uvimba wolwazi uza kunceda ekuchongeni, ekuhlumiseni nasekuphuhliseni italente entsha ukuba ibe ziindwalutho zomculo noonozakuzaku bexa elizayo.

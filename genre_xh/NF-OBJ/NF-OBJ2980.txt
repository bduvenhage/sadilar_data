Inkcazelo
Qho ngenyanga, oogqirha bombuso kulo lonke eli laseMzantsi Afrika bathumela iingxelo zezifo zezilwanyana kuBaqondisi babo baMaphondo beeNkonzo zeZilwanyana. Ezi ngxelo ziyaphononongwa, zize ke zithunyelwe kwiCandelo lelizwe loBuqondisi beMpilo yeZilwanyana (Directorate Animal Health) e-Pretoria, zithi ke apho zifakwe kuvimba ogcina inkcazelo. Iingxelo zenyanga zezifo zezilwanyana ziqulungqwa ngokusebenzisa le nkcazelo. Ezi ngxelo zithunyelwa elizweni nakumazwe ehlabathi kubabandakanyeki nemibutho enomnqweno.

Ukufumana Uncedo Lwezomthetho (IiNkonzo)
Ukuba awunako ukuhlawula igqwetha, ungafumana uncedo lwezomthetho (uncedo ngezemali kwimirhumo yamagqwetha). Kuya kufuneka uphumelele uvavanyo olubizwa ngokuba yi-'means test', oko kukuthi, kufuneka ubonise ukuba urhola imali ngenyanga engaphantsi kwexabiso elimiswe yiBhodi yoNcedo lwaseMthethweni. Eli xabiso liyatshintsha xa ungatshatanga okanye utshatile kwaye likwatshintsha ngokuhamba kwamaxesha. Kufuneka ... &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

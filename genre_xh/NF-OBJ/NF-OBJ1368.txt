iNkonzo Yengeniso yoMzantsi Afrika
UMBHALI: iNkonzo Yengeniso yoMzantsi Afrika



ISISHWANKATHELO

Ezi zizikhokelo eziza kukuncedisa ugcwalise iimpepha zakho zonyaka werhafu u-2005/06. Zisixhenxe ke iintlobo zezi mpepha zineenkcukacha zomhlawuli-rhafu kufuneka zingenisiwe, yaye zahlulwe ngokwemibala.
* IT12SS - iphetshana elimbala u-orenji lelabahlawuli berhafu abafumana umvuzo kodwa bengenazibonelelo ekunokwenziwa kuzo amabango athile
* IT12S - iphetshana eliluhlaza okomnquma lelabahlawuli berhafu abafumana umvuzo ze bafumane nezibonelelo ekunokwenziwa kuzo amabango ezibonelelo ezithile
* IT12B - iphetshana elilubhelu lelabahlawuli berhafu abafumene ingeniso ngenxa yokutyala iimali, kwanakubaphathi beenkampani kwanamalungu eenkampani ezinzinzi
* IT12BU - iphetshana elibomvu elinikwa abahlawuli berhafu abafumana ingeniso yokuthengisa neyokuqhuba iifama
* IT12EI - iphetshana elibu-orenji elinikwa abahlawuli berhafu abamkelwe njengamaziko axolelekileyo ukuba angahlawuli rhafu
* IT12TR - iphetshana elimdaka elinikwa indibanisela yeenkampani
* IT14 - iphetshana eliluhlaza elinikwa iinkampani neenkampani ezincinci

Inkcazelo

Ipemethe yenkqubo yokutshintshiselana ngabafundi yipemethe enikwa umfundi welizwe langaphandle ekufanele athabathe inxaxheba kwiprojekthi okanye kwinkqubo ethile kwiziko lemfundo laseMzantsi Afrika elikwinqanaba lesithathu 

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Yenza isicelo kwindlu yozakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiziko elimele uMzantsi Afrika.
Hambisa incwadi esuka kwiSebe leMfundo okanye kwiziko loluntu lemfundo ephakamileyo eMzantsi Afrika:

engqina ukuba eli ziko lilo eliququzelela okanye eliphethe le nkqubo
echaza imisebenzi yale nkqubo kunye nexesha eliyakuthatyathwa yile nkqubo
engqina ukuba eli ziko liyakuthabatha uxanduva olupheleleyo malunga nalo mfundi ngelixa ekwiRiphabhlikhi kwaye umfundi wamkelelwe ukuba abhaliswe.

Hambisa incwadi esuka kwiziko elithile lelizwe langaphandle:

engqina iinkcukacha zomfundi, kubandakanywa ubhaliso lomfundi kwiziko lemfundo ekwinqanaba lesithathu kwilizwe langaphandle
engqina umhla eyakuqala ngawo le nkqubo eMzantsi Afrika.

Inkcazelo

Abantu abanqwenela ukusebenza njengoososayensi bezendalo bafuneka ukuba babhalise kwiBhunga laseMzantsi Afrika leZifundo zoQeqesho lobuNzululwazi bezeNdalo, ekuthiwa yi-South African Council for Natural Scientific Professions . IBhunga linezinjongo zilandelayo:

ukuphakamisa ukusetyenziswa kwezifundo zoqeqesho lwenzululwazi  yezendalo eMzantsi Afrika;
ukulawula umgangatho wokuziphatha kwasemsebenzini koososayensi bezendalo abaqeqeshiweyo, oososayensi bezendalo abangabalingwa kunye noososayensi okanye oososayensi bezendalo abaqinisekisiweyo;
ukubeka iliso kumgangatho wemfundo noqeqesho loososayensi bezendalo, kunye
nokuvuma imfundo noqeqesho oluyimfuneko phambi kokuba kubhaliswe ngokoMthetho waMaziko aseMzantsi Afrika weZifundo noQeqesho oluLodwa lweNzululwazi, ekuthiwa yi-Academy of Science of South Africa Act, 2001 .

Abantu abangabhaliswanga abanako ukwenza umsebenzi ochongelwe abantu ababhalisiweyo.

Kutheni kufanele ubhalise?

Ukuze wenze umsebenzi wezifundo zoqeqesho
Ukuze usebenzise igama eligunyazisiweyo kunye netayitile ethi 'usosayensi wezendalo'  okanye ufake isishunqulelo esiqingqiweyo emva kwegama lakho 
Ukuze uqhube umsebenzi ngokwesikhundla sokuba yikhonsalthenti
Ukuze uxhamle ubume bemfundo yoqeqesho kwaye ubandakanyeke kwiinzuzo ezinikwa ngamaziko ezemali
Ukuze uxhamle ukuxhaswa yi-SACNASP kunye nokuqinisekiswa okungakheth'icala kolwazi nezakhono zakho
Ukuze kukhuselwe kwaye komelezwe ezi zifundo zoqeqesho

Ziintoni ezifunekayo ukuze ubhaliswe?

Ukuze ubhalisele ukubangusosayensi wezendalo oqeqeshiweyo – kufuneka ubenesidanga  seminyaka emine okanye idiploma yeminyaka emine, idibane namava weminyaka emithathu
Ukuze ubhalisele ukubangusosayensi wezendalo ongumlingwa – kufuneka ubenesidanga seminyaka emine okanye idiploma yeminyaka emine, ungabinamava
Ukuze ubhalisele ukubangusosayensi wezendalo oqinisekisiweyo – kufuneka ubenesidanga seminyaka emithathu okanye idiploma yeminyaka emithathu, ubenama onyaka omnye

Amacandelo obhaliso ngala alandelayo:

USosayensi wezeNdalo oQeqeshiweyo, ekuthiwa yi-Professional Natural Scientist 
USosayensi wezeNdalo onguMlingwa, ekuthiwa yi-Candidate Natural Scientist 
USosayensi wezeNdalo oQinisekisiweyo, ekuthiwa yi-Certificated Natural Scientist 

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwi-South African Council for Natural Scientific Professions.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa ifom yesicelo efumaneka kwiBhunga
Faka kwisicelo sakho intlawulo eqingqiweyo yobhaliso. .

IBhunga liyakukubhalisa kwaye likhuphe isatifiketi sobhaliso ukubangaba lanelisekile ukuba unazo iimfuneko ezibandakanyekayo zemfundo namava njengoko kuqulungqwe liBhunga.

Kuyakubanjwa udliwano-ndlebe nawe ubuqu ligqiza labantu ababini nangaphezulu, abatyunjwe yiKomiti ebandakanyekayo ye-SACNASP yokuCebisa yeZifundo zoQeqesho ekuthiwa yi-Professional Advisory Committee .
Zonke izicelo kufuneka zivavanywe yi-PAC, zivunywe yiKomiti yoBhaliso ye-SACNASP, kwaye ziqinisekiswe ukuvunywa okugunyazisiweyo liBhunga elipheleleyo.
Isiphumo sovavanyo asiyikwaziswa kude kubesemva kwentlanganiso yeBhunga.
IBhunga lingala ukubhalisa isicelo phantsi kwezi meko zilandelayo:

Ukubangaba, emva kokuqalisa koMgaqo-siseko weRiphabhlikhi yaseMzantsi Afrika, 1996, umenzi-sicelo ufunyaniswe enetyala lokwaphula umthetho, ngaphakathi kwiRiphabhlikhi okanye kwenye indawo, waze wagwetyelwa ukuvalelwa entolongweni isithuba esidlula iinyanga ezintathu, okanye ifayini endaweni yokuvalelwa entolongweni.
Ukubangaba iNkundla ePhakamileyo ibhengeze umenzi-sicelo ukuba akamanga kakuhle engqondweni okanye uphazamisekile ngokwengqondo, okanye ukubangaba uvalelwe phantsi koMthetho weMpilo yeNgqondo, ekuthiwa yi-Mental Health Act, 1973 .
Ukubangaba ubhaliso lomenzi-sicelo lunqunyanyisiwe ngenxa yaso nasiphina isohlwayo asiwiselweyo phantsi kwalo Mthetho.
Ukubangaba umenzi-sicelo ususwe esikhundleni sokuthembeka, ngenxa yokuziphatha okungafanelekanga. .
Ukubangaba umenzi-sicelo ungumntu ongakwaziyo ukuhlawula amatyala nongabuyiselwanga kwimeko yesiqhelo, kwaye oko kungakwazi ukuhlawula amatyala kwabangelwa kukungakhathali kwakhe okanye ukungabinalwazi nezakhono zokwenza umsebenzi owela kwimiba echatshazelwa licandelo enzela lona isicelo sobhaliso.

IBhunga liyakukwazisa ngencwadi ebhaliweyo ukubangaba isicelo sakho sobhaliso saliwe
Ungafaka isibheno esiphikisa isigqibo seBhunga, kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama 30.

Kufuneka wenze isicelo sokuvuselela ubhaliso lwakho kwiBhunga, okungenani kwiinyanga ezintathu phambi kokuphelelwa kobhaliso lwakho.

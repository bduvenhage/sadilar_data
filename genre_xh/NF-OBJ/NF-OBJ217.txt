Inkcazelo
Iphepha-mvume lokuqhuba linika umntu igunya lokuqhuba imoto. Eli phepha-mvume liqulethe: Inkcukacha ezixela ukba umqhubi ukulungele ukuhamba endleleni. Kufuneka ukuba ube neminyaka elishumi elinesibhozo nangaphezulu ukze ukwazi ukwenza esi sicelo.
Entla
Inkqubo emayilandelwe
o iphepha-mvume lokufunda ukuqhuba o incwadi yesaziso sakho okanye incwadi yokundwendwela o ifoto ezine ezimnyama namhlophe.
Uvavanyo lwamehlo luzakwenziwa ngaphambi kokubhaliswa kosuku lokuvavanyelwa iphepha-mvume lokuqhuba (Luthathele ingqalelo usuku lovavanyo).
Bhatala imali emisiweyo.

Inkcazelo

Iinkonzo ii-reserved postal services zibandakanya zonke iileta, ii-postcards, izinto eziprintiweyo , iipasile ezincinane nezinye izinto eziposwayo ezizi- postal articles. Ilayisensi inika umnininyo  ilungelo lokusebenza njengenkonzo yezeposi eyenza umsebenzi othile  kwaye ibaphantsi kolawulo lwemiqathango yomthetho  ekubhekiswe kuzo kwiSahluko 16 soMthetho iPostal Services Act, 1998.

Nceda uqaphele: Ukukhutshwa kwelayisensi yemimandla egcinelwe iinjongo ezithile  kuya kwenzeka kuphela xa uMphathiswa ebhengeze isimemo sokuba kwenziwe izicelo. Kungokunje, iPosi yaseMzantsi Afrika  kuphela iyinto enobuntu enamalungelo ngokomthetho enelayisensi yokusebenza . Akukho mntu unokusebenza njengenkonzo yezeposi eyenza umsebenzi othile  ngaphandle kokuba abe nelayisensi yokwenza njalo.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Zalisa ifom yokwenza isicelo ngokuprinta ngoonobumba abakhulu.
Ngenisa ifom yokwenza isicelo kwiSebe lezoNxibelelwano .
Iifom zokwenza isicelo ezingazaliswanga ngokupheleleyo ziya kubuyiselwa kumenzi-sicelo kwaye akukho mntu uya kuthwaliswa uxanduva ngenxa yako nakuphi na ukungaphatheki kakuhle okumalunga noku.

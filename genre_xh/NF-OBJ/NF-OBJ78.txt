Inkcazelo
Nawuphina umntu owayeke wabangummi, ngokuzalwa okanye ngokomnombo, owathi wabuyeka ubumi baseMzantsi Afrika okanye obu bumi baseMzantsi Afrika baziphelela ngokwabo, angenza isicelo sokuphinda abengummi waseMzansti Afrika.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Hambisa incwadi engqina ukuba umenzi-sicelo ngoku uhlala eMzantsi Afrika.
Hambisa ikopi yencwadi yesazisi yomenzi-sicelo.
Hambisa ikopi yesatifiketi somtshato, ukuba oko kuyabandakanyeka.

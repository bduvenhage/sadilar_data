Inkcazelo
I-visa luphawu olugunyazisiweyo olubekwa kwipaspoti esebenzisekayo yomntu welizwe langaphandle, olunika loo mntu welizwe langaphandle igunya lokungena eMzantsi Afrika.
ifunyanwe phambi kokuba umenzi-sicelo alishiye ilizwe lakhe endulukela eMzantsi Afrika ifunyanwe kwilizwe langaphandle, kwaye akunakwenziwa isicelo sayo kumasango okungena aseMzantsi Afrika kwenziwe isicelo sayo kwelo lizwe, kwindlu yozakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiziko elimele uMzantsi Afrika, kumazwe apho uMzantsi Afrika umelweyo kuwo kwenziwe isicelo sayo kwindlu yozakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika ekufuphi okanye kwiziko elimele uMzantsi Afrika elikufuphi, ukubangaba uMzantsi Afrika awumelwanga elizweni lakho. Oku kuyabandakanyeka nakubantu abaneepaspoti zozakuzelwano lwamazwe nezigunyazisiweyo abafuna iipemethe zozakuzelwano phakathi kwamazwe.

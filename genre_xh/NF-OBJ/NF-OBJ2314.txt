Iindlela Zokuseka Iintlobo Ezahlukeneyo Zamashishini Ezotyelelo





Inkqubo Yophuhliso Loshishino Lwezokhenketho (Department of Economic Development and Tourism, uRhulumente Wephondo leNtshona Kapa)



ISISHWANKATHELO

Isicatshulwana esisuka kupapasho oluthi "Facilitating Entrepreneurship in Tourism", esi sikhokelo sijoliswe ekuncedeni abanini-mashishini ezotyelelo ukuba baqale amashishini kwicandelo lezotyelelo. Siquka iinkcukacha ngokufumanisa ngemimiselo yoshishino eyahlukeneyo ze iqinisekise futhi ukuba ishishini lakho liyayithobela.



UMTHAMO:

* Indlela Yokuseka iRestaurant, iNdlu Yezidlo okanye iTavern
* Indlela Yokuseka Ishishini Elinikezela Ngendawo Yokulala Nesidlo Sakusasa okanye iNdlu Yeendwendwe
* Indlela Yokuseka Inkonzo Yokukhokela Abatyeleli
* Iindlela Zokuseka Ishishini Lokuthutha Abatyeleli

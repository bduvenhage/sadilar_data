Inkcazelo

Injongo yale nkonzo kukwenza isicelo sokophulelwa imali nokubuyiselwa imali kumrhumo wokurhafela iimveliso ezingeniswe elizweni zokwenza, ukugcina nokuqhubekisela kwimveliso, ukugqibezela, ukufaka izinto eziyimfuneko okanye ukupakisha iimveliso ezijoliswe ekubeni zithunyelwe kumazwe angaphandle.

Ipemethe iyimfuneko ukuze ungenise iimveliso ngokwalo mmiselo, kwaye kufuneka yenzelwe isicelo ngokugcwalisa ifom yesicelo efunyanwa kwiKomishoni yokuLawulwa koRhwebo lwaMazwe eHlabathi, ekuthiwa yi-International Trade Administration Commission .

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwi International Trade Administration Commission.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khuphela oluxhwebhu lunezikhewu kwi, 470.03/00.00/01.00.
Khupha ikopi eqinisekisiweyo yencwadi yakho yesazisi.
Khupha ikopi eqinisekisiweyo yepemethe yakho yokuthumela impahla nezinto kumazwe angaphandle.

Umthetho osetyenziswayo

Customs and Excise Act, 1964  UMthetho weMirhumo yeMpahla eNgena eLizweni kunye neRhafu yeMpahla neZinto kunye neeLayisensi ezithile.

Inkcazelo

Xa unemoto engazange ibhaliswe nengenamaphepha-mvume ofuna ukuyiqhuba endleleni kawonke-wonke ngenjongo yokuya kuyibhalisa, ukuyenzela iphepha-mvume,ukuyihambisa kwabarhweba ngeemoto, evavanya imoto, eyiqhuba imoto ukuya nokubuya apho ilungiswa khona,ukuya kubacwaningi beemoto okanye ukuyoyithatha kumthengi ohluleka ukuyibhatala, kufuneka ufumane imvume yexeshana yokuyiqhuba.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa ifomu lesicelo TSP1, yesicelo semvume yethutyana kwi ofisi yamagosa endlela ekufutshane nawe okanye iziphathi-mandla zobhaliso.
Thumela ikopi yencwadi yesazisi.
Bhatala imali ebekiweyo.

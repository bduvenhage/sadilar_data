Inkcazelo

Onke amayeza ezolimo angeniswa elizweni, awenziweyo, avelisiweyo okanye athengiswayo apha eMzantsi Afrika kufuneka abhaliswe ku-Registrar : Act 36 of 1947 .

Minyaka le iofisi yoMbhalisi ithumela iileta malunga nohlaziyo neefomu zesicelo kubo bonke abanobhaliso.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Uhlaziyo:

zalisa ifomu yesicelo  ngeekopi ezintathu uze ubhale ngokwahlukanisa oonobumba ubhale ngoonobumba abakhulu kuphela
thumela kuMbhalisi: Act 36 of 1947 
umhla wokugqibela wokuhlaziya ngowama-30 Juni.

Ukucima:

zalisa iFomu B ngeekopi ezintathu uze ubhale ngokwahlukanisa oonobumba usebenzisa oonobumba abakhulu
thumela isiqinisekiso setsusa sokubhalisa

Iinkcukacha zomenzi wesicelo:

zalisa iFomu C ukuhlaziya irekhodi yombhalisi yeenkcukacha zonxibelelwano, umzkl, idilesi, inombolo yefowuni, nomntu woqhagamshelwano.

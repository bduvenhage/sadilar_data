Inkcazelo
UMhlaba weZolimo oleleyo woMbuso uyaqeshiswa ngeenjongo zolimo ngokunika okanye ngokunganiki ithuba lokukhetha ukuwuthenga, kuxhomekeke kubume balo mhlaba (ngaba kukho isibango na kulo mhlaba). Izivumelwano zokuqeshisa ezinika ithuba lokuthenga ziyahlaziyeka qho emva kweminyaka emithathu.
Entla
Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Xa umhlaba wezolimo woMbuso ulungele ukuqeshiswa, iSebe leZolimo liyazisa ngawo ngokuwu-advethayiza ngokwezaziso kwiindawo ezibonakala kakuhle kwingingqi, ezifana nee-ofisi zoomantyi, izikhululo zamapolisa nakumaphephandaba.
Yenza isicelo kulo mntu uxelwe kwisaziso.

Uncedo Kubanini-mashishini Abasakhulayo




INKCAZELO:

Ngo-2001/2002 abafundi abangama-3000 abavela kwizikolo ezingama-64 baye baziswa ngoqeqesho loshishino.

Aba banikezeli ngeenkonzo balandelayo nabanceda abanini-mashishini abasakhulayo ngenkxaso kaRhulumente wePhondo.
* IJunior Archievement SA
* Umsobomvu Youth Fund
* I-Ziphelele



JUNIOR ARCHIEVEMENT SA

www.jasa.org.za

I-JASA iqhuba iinkqubo ezintathu ikhuthaza ubunini bamashishini kulutsha.

I-Enterprise Dynamics Programme
I-EDP isekelezwe ezikolweni yaye ilungele abafundi ukusuka kwiNqanaba lokuqala ukuya kwele-12. Ootitshala basebenzisa imidlalo, imisebenzi yokulinganisa, iinkqubo nemidlalo yokulinganisa ukunceda ulutsha lube nokuqonda uqoqosho noshishino.

Mini Enterprise Programme
Le nkqubo ijoliswe ekunikezeleni uqeqesho loshishino nobuchule ngobomi kubafundi bezikolo zamabanga aphezulu. Ezi zakhono ziza kubanceda ukuba bathabathe inxaxheba kwingqesho yequmrhu okanye yoshishino kunye/okanye ukuqalisa awabo amashishini. Abafundi baqokelelwa kwiindawo ezineemeko ezahlukeneyo ngokwenkcubeko. Emva kokuthabatha inxaxheba enkqubeni, abafundi bafumana ithuba lokubhala uviwo loshishini lwamazwe ngamazwe oluqhutywa yiYunivesithi yaseCambridge, eUnited Kingdom.

I-Business Establishment and Sustainability Programme (BESP)
I-BESP inceda abantu abatsha abangagqibanga ukufunda bengenakufumana misebenzi. Le nkqubo iqeqesha ulutsha ukuba luzidalele amathuba emisebenzi ze lukwazi nokuwagcina. Ezinye iinkonzo ezinikezelwayo ziquka:

* uphuhliso lwezicwangciso zeshishini
* ukunceda bafumane imali-mboleko ebhankini
* ukusekwa kweshishini
* ukunika iingcebiso.

UMSOBOMVU YOUTH FUND

www.uyf.org.za

Ingxowa-mali yoLutsha, uMsobomvu, inceda ukudala amathuba emisebenzi nobunini-mashishini kulutsha ngokuyila, ukuxhasa ngezimali nokunika inkxaso kwiinkqubo ezisebenzayo. Le ngxowa-mali yasekwa nguRhulumente womZantsi Afrika ukukhawulelana nentswela-msebenzi kulutsha lweli.

nguMsobomvu yiBusiness Development Support Voucher Programme
Enye yeenkqubo eziqhutywa nguMsobomvu yiBusiness Development Support Voucher Programme. Isiboniso selungelo lokwamkela isipho luxwebhu olunikezelwa kubaxumi abakhethiweyo nolubanika ilungelo lokuthenga iinkqubo zenkxaso yoshishino kubanikezeli beenkonzo abavunyiweyo. Iqela ekujoliswe kulo ngale nkqubo yesi siboniso ikakhulu lulutsha oluphuma esikolweni nolubudala buphakathi kwe-18 nama-35 eminyaka, oludinga iinkqubo zenkxaso yoshishino ukuqala okanye ukuphucula amashishini alo kumacandelo awohlukeneyo, kuquka nokhenketho.

Kuqala, le nkqubo yesiboniso iza kuvavanywa kumaPhondo amahlunu, kuquka neNtshona Kapa.

Ukufumana ulwazi oluthe vetshe malunga nenkqubo yeleNtshona Kapa, qhagamshelana neZiko leeNkonzo zoPhuhliso kule nombolo: 021 683 1350.

Ulwazi Neengcebiso Ezifumaneka Kwikhompyutha
Isikhundla Sonxibelelwano Solutsha soMsobomvu siqulethe ulwazi ngokukwazi ukuphatha ishishini futhi singakunceda ufumanise:
* ukuba ngaba ungakwazi na ukumelana nokuba ngumnini weshishini
* ukuba ungasenza njani na isicwangciso soshishino
* ukuba ungawabhaqa njani na amathuba oshishino
* nokuba ungalibhalisa njani futhi uliqale njani na ishishi lakho


Umsobomvu ukwavelisele abanini bamashishini abasebatsha iincwadana ezinika umkhomba-ndlela ezithi:
* From Idea to Opportunity: A Guide for Young People
* Getting Business Finance: A Guide for Young Entrepreneurs
* Networking Your Way to Success: A Guide for Young Entrepreneurs
* Starting a Co-operative: A Guide for Young People
* Starting Your Own Business: A Guide for Young Entrepreneurs
* Writing a Business Plan: A Guide for Young Entrepreneurs

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Ukuba umdala kakhulu okanye uyagula kakhulu ukuba ungahamba uye e-ofisini, cela umntu ukuba akwenzele isicelo sokuba undwendwelwe ekhaya, egameni lakho. Loo mntu kufuneka eze neleta evela kuwe okanye ingxelo kagqirha (doctor's note) echaza isizathu sokuba ungakwazi ukuya e-ofisini.
Zalisa ifom yesicelo yakho phambi kwegosa elivela kwiSebe.
o ukuba ungaphantsi kweminyaka eyi-60, uvavanyo lonyango okanye ingxelo echaza ukuba akunakho ukusebenza.
Lamaphepha alandelayo ayafuneka ukuze ufake isicelo, xa incwadi yesazisi okanye isitifiketi singekho:
Inkcazelo malunga nemeko yakho yomtshato:
o ukuba akutshatanga, i-afidavithi (affidavit) eyingxelo efungelweyo echaza ukuba akutshatanga o ukuba utshatile, isatifikethi sakho somtshato o ukuba uwuqhawule umtshato, umyalelo wakho wokuqhawula umtshato o ukuba umlingane wakho wasweleka, isatifikethi sokusweleka somlingane wakho.
Fumana irisithi uyigcine njengobungqina bokuba wenze isicelo.
Uya kuchazelwa ngento ebhaliweyo ukuba ngaba isicelo sakho sibe yimpumelelo okanye akunjalo.
Ukuba isicelo sakho asivunywanga, i-ofisi yokhuseleko loluntu iya kukwazisa ngento ebhaliweyo ecacisa izizathu zokuba kungani na ukuba isicelo sakho singabanga yimpumelelo.
Yenza isibheno kwithuba leentsuku eziyi-90 zokufumana kwakho isaziso malunga nesiphumo sesicelo sakho.

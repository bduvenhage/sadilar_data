Inkcazelo

Zonke izityalo ezibulala iintsholongwane ezisebenzayo apha eMzantsi Afrika kufuneka zibhaliswe kuMbhalisi: Act 36 of 1947 . Isityalo esibulala iintsholongwane sisetyenziswa ukubulala iintsholongwane kumathambo okanye ezinye izinto ezivela kwizilwanyana.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khupha ifomu yesicelo kwi-inthanethi uze uyizalise ngeekopi ezintathu. Ifomu kananjalo ingacelwa kuMbhalisi: Act 36 of 1947 . Intsayino yokwenene iyafuneka kwifomu nganye ehlukileyo.
Fakela intlawulo yobhaliso. Iitsheki neposoda kufuneka zihlawulwe ku-Director –General: Agriculture.

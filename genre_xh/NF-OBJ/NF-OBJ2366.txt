Amaphetshana enkcazelo ase-Elsenburg




INKCAZELO:

ISebe lezoLimo laseNtshona Koloni liye laqulunqa imiqulwana yenkcazelo emininzi apho kunikwa khona iinkcazelwana ezimfutshane kwiinkalo ngeenkalo zezolimo.

Iphetshana ngalinye kula maphetshana enkcazelo angamashumi alithoba anesine (94) lifumaneka ngesiBhulu, ngesiNgesi nagesiXhosa, yaye libhalwe ngolwimi oluqondakala lula. Nakubeni ejoliswe kakhulu kubalimi abasaqalayo, abalimi abasele benamava bangavuna lukhulu nabo kula maphetshana zenkcazelo. Kusetyenziswe nemifanekiso kuzo zonke ngeenjongo zokuzenza ziqondakale msinyane.La maphetshana enkcazelo ke akho phantsi kwezi nkalo zilandelayo:
* Izilwanyana: Izifo
* Izilwanyana: Ukuxheshisa
* Izilwanyana: Ulawulo
* Izilwanyana: Iintlobo
* Izilimo: Imifuno
* Izilimo: Ezinye
* Ulawulo lwemali
* Ukulima iziqhamo
* Ukunkcenkceshela
* Ukusetyenziswa kwemitshini
* Umhlaba
* Imithi



IMIYALELO:

Jonga amaphetshana enkcazelo simahla apha www.elsenburg.com okanye uthenge amaphetshana eenkcukacha zemiboniso njengoko zokwenza imiboniso.

IXABISO:

Onke amaphetshana enkcazelo kuvimba weenkcukacha wase-Elsenburg, i-Elsenburg website.

Izilayidi zemibala zephetshana ngalinye kula maphetshana enkcazelo lingathengwa ngokukodwa ngofuna ukulisebenzisa kwintetho-nkcazelo, ngala maxabiso alandelayo:
Amancinane (10 izilayidi okanye ngaphantsi) - R100.00
Aphakathi (11- 25 izilayidi) - R170.00
Amakhulu (26 izilayidi okanye ngaphezulu) - R240.00

Ngeli xabiso libonisiweyo uya kufumana ZONKE izilayidi zephetshana lenkcazelo elithile. Ngamanye amazwi ke, iphetshana lenkcazelo elinezilayidi ezisibhozo liya kukudla i-R100, ngelixa iphetshana lenkcazelo elinezilayidi ezingama-20 lona liya kukudla i-R170. Eli xabiso libonisiweyo alizibandakanyi iindleko zokuwathumela.
Kananjalo, phezu kwezi zilayidi uya kufumana nekopi yephetshana eli simahla ibhalwe ngesiBhulu, ngesiNgesi nangesiXhossa.

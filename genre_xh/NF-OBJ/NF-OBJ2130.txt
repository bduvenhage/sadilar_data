Ulwazi Ngamacandelo oRhwebo




INKCAZELO:

Ungaluzuza ulwazi ngamashishini nangamacandelo orhwebo aphathelele kwiPhondo kunye neSizwe kumaSebe ahluka-hlukeneyo kaRhulumente kwakunye neendawo zoLuntu ezikhoyo.

IWesgro iye yathabatha izifundo ekuthiwa yi-izifundo zangasese ngamacandelo amaninzi kwiNtshona-Koloni yaye kwakhona yavelisa amaphepha aqulethe inyaniso alula ukuwafunda eziphathelele kwezi ngxelo.

I-Industrial Development Corporation of South Africa lamacandelo oshishino obuchule nawo anikezela ngolwazi kwi kwakunye noncedo kula macandelo alandelayo:

* Urhwebo lwezomhlaba
* amachiza
* ugunyaziso
* ushishino ngezimbiwa nezihombisi ezinamatye anqabileyo
* imali yamazwe ngamazwe
* izinyithi, izinto ezikhwelwayo, kunye nomatshini
* uPhuhliso loLuntu olungama-Afrika aseMzantsi
* Urhwebo lolwazi lobugcisa
* Impahla eyolukiweyo, impahla, izikhumba kwakunye nezihlangu
* Ukhenketho
* Imali yamashishini athengisela amanye
* Umthi, iphepha nokunye
* Ezeendaba, nemiboniso bhanya-bhanya.

U-I-Trade and Investment South Africa inikezela ngolwazi oluphathelele kwinkqubo yeli candelo kwezomhlaba, icandelo lezinto ezihamba ngomatshini, ulwazi kwakunye necandelo lezonxulumano lobugcisa kunye necandelo lempahla eyolukiweyo kwakunye nempahla jikelele.
Njengenxenye yeenkonzo zenkxaso yoshishino, i- Business Referral and Information Network (BRAIN) zinika ulwazi kumacandelo amaninzi.

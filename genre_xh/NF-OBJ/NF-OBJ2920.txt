Inkcazelo
Abantu okanye imibutho inelungelo lokwenza isicelo se namba pleyiti yesimntwiso xa befuna ukusebenzisa amagama abo okanye awemibutho njengamanani okubhaliswa kwemoto. Eli nani limntwisiweyo libonakaliswa ngendlela efanayo nenani lobhaliso lwemoto lesiqhelo. Inani liba yimpahla yomntu inamba pleyiti ebhaliswe ngaye.
Inamba pleyiti ebhaliswe ngegama lomntu okanye le nkampani kufuneka ilandele yonke imiqathango elandelwa xa kubhaliswa inamba pleyiti yesiqhelo (umzekelo kufuneka ibe nelogo yephondo).

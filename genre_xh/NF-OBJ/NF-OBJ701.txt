Ulwazi Kubantu Abafuna Ukurhweba




INKCAZELO:

Isebe lezeNtengiso noShishino libonelela ngolwazi oluneenkcukacha kwikhompyutha kubantu abafuna ukurhweba noMzantsi Afrika.

Oku kuquka ulwazi ukuba:
* Kutheni kufuneka kurhwetyiwe noMzantsi Afrika
* Imithetho Elawula Urhwebo
* Ukuhlala Nokusebenza eMzantsi Afrika
* Ukwenza Ushishino eMzantsi Afrika
* Amanyathelo Enkuthazo.

I-Wesgro ibonelela ngolwazi kubantu abanomdla ekurhwebeni neNtshona Koloni. Olu lwazi luquka ukujongwa nolwazi oluneenkcukacha nge:
* Uqoqosho lweNtshona Koloni
* Amathuba OrhweboAmacandelo Oshishino
* Indawo Yokushishina yeNtshona Koloni.

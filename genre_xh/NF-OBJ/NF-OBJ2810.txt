Inkcazelo

Zonke izimoto ezisebenzisa imigwaqo yomphakathi kumele zihlolwe ukuthi ukuze kuqinisekiswe ukuthi zilungele ukuhamba emgwaqweni. Kungumsebenzi womnikazi wemoto ukuthi amikise imoto yakhe iyohlolwa. Ukuhlolwa kwemoto kungenziwa esenta kahulumeni noma esenta ezimele.

isitifiketi sokubhaliswa semoto
incwadi yomazisi yakho.



Khokha imali enqunyiwe.

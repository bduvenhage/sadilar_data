Inkcazelo

Lakuba iqumrhu elingavulelekanga ekuthiwa yi-close corporation  licinyiwe kubhaliso, singenziwa isicelo sokuba liphinde libuyiselwe kubhaliso, kodwa ke kuya kufuneka kulandelwe imigaqo efanelekileyo.

Kufuneka kuqale kuqondwe ubume be-CC kwi-Ofisi yoBhaliso lweeNkampani kunye neMpahla neZinto eziyiMveliso yeNgqondo ekuthiwa yi-Companies and Intellectual Property Registration Office  kuba kungenzeka ukuba i-South African Revenue Service  ibe ifake isicelo sokucinywa kobhaliso leli qumrhu lingavulelekanga.

Kufuneka kufakwe i-R150 efakelwa uvimba wophawu lweekhastoma kwaye iya kuxhuzulwa kwi-akhawunti yekhastoma emva kokungeniswa kwefom.

Malunga neenkcukacha ezithe vetshe, yiya Companies and Intellectual Property Registration Office.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Zalisa ifom yesicelo ekuthiwa yi-CK3.
Hambisa incwadi ebhaliweyo nefungelweyo  esayinwe yaze yagximfizwa isitampu nguMkomishina weZifungo, eyenziwe nguye nawuphi na umntu onomnqweno echaza izizathu zokwenza isicelo sokubuyisela ubhaliso lwe-CC.


Ukubangaba iqumrhu elingavulelekanga linayo nayiphi na ipropati emiyo, fumana ungqino olubhaliweyo olwenziwe liSebe leMisebenzi yoLwakhiwo kwiindawo zoLuntu naliCandelo loNondyebo weLizwe, sokuba abakuchasi ukubuyiselwa kobhaliso lwequmrhu elingavulelekanga.

Loo njongo kuya kufuneka ikhutshelwe isibhengezo sokwazisa kwiphephandaba lendawo ukuze kuzanywe ukuqonda iimfuno neminqweno yoluntu malunga nokubuyiswa kobhaliso lwequmrhu elingavulelekanga.

Ukubangaba kukho iinkcaso eziveziweyo, umPhathi woBhaliso lweeNkampani kuya kufuneka aziswe ngezo nkcaso kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama 30 zokucinywa kobhaliso.

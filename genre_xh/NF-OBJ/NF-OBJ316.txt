Ulwazi Lwenzuzo Yemveliso Ephuma Ngaphandle




INKCAZELO:

ISebe Labongameli liyayithakazelela into yokusasaza ulwazi ngeenkqubo zenzuzo yemvelisa eya ngaphandle, ingakumbi ulwazi lwenzuzo yorhwebo olutsha lwesizwe.Olunye uyilo lwenzuzo ekhoyo ibandakanya:
* INkqubo Yentengiso yeMveliso Ephuma Ngaphandle kwakunye neNkqubo Yoncedo Kurhweb (DTI)
* Izibonelelo Zempahla Ekhulayo (DTI)
* Ezezimali -Imali Yamazwe Ngamazwe (IDC)
* Izimali - SADC (IDC).



IMIYALELO:

Ungazuza iinkcukacha zolwazi lwezi nkqubo zenzuzo zidwelisiwe kwi-websayithi i- DTI.

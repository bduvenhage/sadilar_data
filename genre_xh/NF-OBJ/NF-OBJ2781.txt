Inkcazelo

Ilungelo lokuvelisa nokuqamba ekuthiwa yi-patent lilungelo elikhethekileyo elinikwayo ngenxa yokuvelisa nokuqamba into ethile. Olo veliso noqambo kufuneka lubeyimveliso okanye inkqubo ezisa indlela entsha yokwenza izinto, okanye enika isisombululo esitsha sobuchule kwingxaki ethile.

I-patent ikhusela olu veliso nolu qambo kumnikazi we-patent. Olu khuselo lunikwa ixesha eliqingqiweyo kuphela, elidla ngokuba yiminyaka engama 20. Ukhuselo lokuvelisa nokuqamba  luthetha ukuba olu veliso alunakwenziwa, lusetyenziswe, lusasazwe okanye luthengiswe, ngokorhwebo, ngaphandle kwemvume yomnikazi.

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi yiya kwi Companies and Intellectual Property Registration Office.

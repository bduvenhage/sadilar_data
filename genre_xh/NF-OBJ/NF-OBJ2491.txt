Inkcazelo

Esi sicelo somyalelo sifakwa kwi-South African Revenue Service  ukuze kucelwe ukuba kuxhuzulwe imali esisigxina yerhafu kwingeniso-mali yomrhafi okanye kwimali ayamkela qho ngonyaka.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Qinisekisa ukuba imibandela yakho yerhafu ime kakuhle phambi kokuba wenze isicelo somyalelo werhafu. I-SARS ayidli ngokuwukhupha umyalelo werhafu ukubangaba kukho iincwadi zokubuyisa zerhafu ongekazifaki okanye unemali ongekayihlawuli yerhafu.

Khupha ezi zinto zilandelayo ukuze uncedise i-SARS ekuvumeni isicelo sakho somyalelo werhafu:

ifom yesicelo eyi-IRP3
i-ID  yakho kunye nenombolo yakho yobungqina berhafu
inombolo yobungqina be-PAYE yabaqashi bakho okanye yabalawuli bengxowa-mali
ingxelo egcweleyo yengeniso-mali nenkcitho. I-ofisi yebhrentshi ye-SARS ingacela ubungqina beenkcitho ezithile phambi kokuba ivume isicelo somyalelo
indlela esetyenzisiweyo  okanye ukubalwa kokubanoxanduva lwerhafu.

Sakuba sivunyiwe isicelo yi-SARS, uyakunikwa ubungqina bokuvunywa kwiintsukwana ezimbalwa, kodwa wona umyalelo werhafu ogunyazisiweyo ungathatha ixesha elinokuya kuthi ga kwiiveki ezine.

Eyona ndlela ilula kakhulu yokwenza isicelo somyalelo werhafu kukusifaka nge- eFiling. Isicelo sakho somyalelo werhafu esenziwe ngale nkonzo sidla ngokuvunywa kwisithuba seeyure ezingama 48 ukubangaba i-ofisi ayidingi bungqina beenkcitho.


Isicelo sokwamkelwa ku-grade R kwisikolo soluntu esibhalisiweyo    



Inkcazelo 

U-Grade R ngunyaka wokuqala wesigaba sesiseko semfundo esekelwe kwiziphumo kwizikolo zoluntu zamabanga aphantsi . Eli banga lilungiselelwe abantwana abaneminyaka yobudala emine nesiqingatha ukuya kwemihlanu nesiqingatha. Abazali kunye nabantu abangabalondolozi babantwana kufuneka babhalise abantwana babo esikolweni phambi komhla wokuvala, ophakathi kuka-Agasti no-Okthobha, wonyaka ophambi kokuba abantwana baqale.

Fa o batla tshedimosetso e e fetang e o e filweng ikgolaganye le Lefapha la Thuto

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Yenza isicelo phambi komhla wokuvala obekwe zizikolo ophakathi kuka-Agasti no-Okthobha.

Bhalisa kwisikolo esikufuphi kuwe.

Hambisa isatifiketi sokuzalwa kunye nekhadi lokugonywa.

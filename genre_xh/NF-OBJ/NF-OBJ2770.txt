Inkcazelo

I-arhente yemvume yokungenisa elizweni ngokuphathelele kwizixhobo zofuzo lwezilwanyana kufuneka ibhaliswe ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement  phambi kokuba ingenise izilwanyana elizweni okanye izixhobo zofuzo ezifana ne-embriyo, ii-ova okanye incindi yobudoda apha eMzantsi Afrika.

Kufuneka i-arhente yokungenisa elizweni ithobele izinto ezilandelayo:

isakhelo kufuneka sibenazo zonke izixhobo ezimiselweyo malunga nokusebenza ngezixhobo zofuzo lwezilwanyana
ilebhu kunye nesakhelo sokuqinisekisa serhafu yokungenisa elizweni kufuneka silandele imiqathango esezantsi emiselweyo
abaqeshi kufuneka bafaneleke ukusebenza ngencindi yobudoda.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khupha ifomu yesicelo kwi-inthanethi uze uyizalise ngokupheleleyo wenze iikopi ezimbini. Ifomu kananjalo ingacelwa ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement .
Izicelo ezingazaliswanga ngokupheleleyo ziya kuphindiswa kumenzi wesicelo.
Isicelo kufuneka siqinisekiswe ngugqirha wezilwanyana ogunyaziswe ukwenza njalo yi-South African Veterinary Authority .
Intlawulo echanekileyo kufuneka ikhaphe isicelo ngasinye. Yenza itsheki okanye iposoda ehlawuleka ku-: The Director-General: Agriculture. Iinkcukacha zebhanki zezi:

Inkcazelo

Ubhaliso lokuhlawula irhafu ekuthiwa yi-value-added tax  kwiNkonzo yeNgeniso-mali yaseMzantsi Afrika ebizwa ngokuba yi-South African Revenue Service , lungacinywa phantsi kwezi meko zilandelayo:

xa ixabiso lesitokhwe sempahla ezihlawulelwa irhafu lihlile laya kutsho ngaphantsi kwesiqingqo se R300 000 ngonyaka
xa uyeka ukuwaqhuba wonke amashishini kwaye ungasayikuqhuba naliphina ishishini kwisithuba seenyanga ezili 12 emva komhla
xa wenze isicelo sobhaliso ngenxa yokulindela ukuqalisa ishishini elitsha, uze ke ube

Nokubangaba awusabhaliselwanga ukuhlawula i-VAT, usafanelekile ukuba uthwaliswe naluphina uxanduva olufumaneke phantsi koMthetho we-Value-Added Tax, wango 1991, ngela xesha wawusabhalisiwe.

Kufuneka wazise i-SARS kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama 21 emva kokuvala onke amashishini.

I-SARS ayisoze ilucime ubhaliso lomniki-nkonzo ukubangaba kukho izizathu eziqiqileyo zokukholelwa ukuba lo mntu uyakuqala ishishini kulo naliphina ixesha kwisithuba seenyanga ezili 12 ukusukela kumhla woko kuyeka.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khupha ifom yesicelo kwi-website uze uyigcwalise, okanye hamba uyokwenza isicelo kwi-ofisi ye-SARS apho ubhaliswe khona.

I-SARS iyakunika isaziso esibhaliweyo kumntu obandakanyekayo malunga nokucinywa kobhaliso okanye ukwaliwa kokucinywa kobhaliso.

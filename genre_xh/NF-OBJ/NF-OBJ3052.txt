Inkcazelo
abalindele izigqebhezana zabo zamaphepha-mvume okuqhuba abenze isicelo sokuvuselela amaphepha-mvume abo okuqhuba abalahlekelwe ngamaphepha-mvume abo okuqhuba.
Eli phepha-mvume liphelelwa lixesha emva kwee nyanga ezintandathu futhi ikhutshwa yiyo nayiphi na indawo yokuvavanyelwa iphepha-mvume lokuqhuba.

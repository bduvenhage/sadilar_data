Inkcazelo
Ubhaliso lusebenziseka ithuba lonyaka omnye ukusukela kumhla wokukhutshwa kwesatifiketi sobhaliso.
Ukuhambisa impahla nezinto ezinobunzima obuyikhilogrem enye okanye nangaphantsi
Ukukhutshwa kwezitampu zokuposa
Ukunikwa kwenkonzo yokuqokelela ecaleni kwendlela kunye neebhokisi zee-adresi.
Ezi aziwabandakanyi amashishini anika iimveliso zawo, afana nommntu othengisa nocwangcisa iintyatyambo, usosilarha okanye ivenkile yegrosari.
Abantu abazimele bucala okanye amashishini azimele bucala, akhululekile enze isicelo selayisensi yokuqhuba inkonzo yeposi engabekelwanga iziko elithile (kuyo nayiphina inkonzo engabandakanywanga kolu luhlu lungentla).
Isicelo sokubhalisa kuMgqaliseli weeNkonzo zePosi ukuze uqhube inkonzo yeposi engabekelwanga iziko elithile, kufuneka siqingqwe ngendlela, kwaye sihambisane nomgaqo, ochazwe nguMphathiswa ngokweziphakamiso ezenziwe nguMgqaliseli (Regulator).
Ubhaliso lusebenziseka ithuba lonyaka omnye ukusukela kumhla wokukhutshwa kwesatifiketi sobhaliso.
Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwiSebe lezoNxibelelwano

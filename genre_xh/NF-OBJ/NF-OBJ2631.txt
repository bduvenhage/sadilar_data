Inkcazelo

Bonke abaniki-nkonzo beenkqubo ze-FET ezinika iikhwalifikheyshini ezigcweleyo kwinqanaba 2 ukuya ku 4 loQingqo lweeKhwalifikheyshini zeLizwe ekuthiwa yi-National Qualifications Framework , kufuneka babhalisele ukubangaMaziko abucala eMfundo noQeqesho oluQhubekayo abizwa ngokuba zii-private Further Education and Training institutions . Abenzi-zicelo kukwafuneka babhaliswe ngokoMthetho weeNkampani, ekuthiwa yi-Companies Act, 1973 .

Amaziko anika iikhosi ezimfutshane, iinkqubo ezinganxulumananga ne-NQF okanye iinxenye zeekhwalifikheyshini, azichatshazelwa lubhaliso ngokomthetho we-FET, ekuthiwa yi-FET Act .

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwiSebe leMfundo.

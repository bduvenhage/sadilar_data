Inkcazelo
Imali eyingeniso yeNgxowa-mali yeMbuyekezo iqulethe ubukhulu becala iimali ezihlawulwe ngabaqeshi ababhaliswe ngokusemthethweni ngokweqondo lepesentenji okanye eqingqiweyo kwiimali eziyingeniso zonyaka zabaqeshwa babo.
UMthetho ubonelela isixa esincinane semali ezihlawulwe ngabaqeshi ukuqinisekisa ukuba intlawulo ayikho ngaphantsi kweendleko zolawulo.

Ingcaciso yabaPhathi









AbaPhathi beSATI bebebambe intlanganiso ngomhla we-12 ngoJuni ka-2008. Apha ngezantsi kukho isishwankathelo seyona mibandela iphambili bekuxoxwa ngayo.



Indebe yeHlabathi ka-2010 kwiBhola ekhatywayo



Luphicotho lwezakhono ebekujoliswe kuzo ngaba-kwaSATI ukuze balungise uvimba wabo weenkcukacha kwicala leetoliki kweli lizwe lethu lonke, emva kokucebi-sana neSebe lezoBulungisa, bafumene abaninzi abantu ababonise ukuba nomdla, kwabaninzi abakulo msebenzi abangawo amalungu, kwabanye abantu abakwiilwimi ezingaqhelekanga. AbakwaSATI baza kuphinda badi-bane neSebe lezoBulungisa ngoJulayi ukuze kuxoxwe nganyathelo elilandelayo.



Uphando ngerhafu



Lusaqhutywa uphando ngerhafu yaye abaPhathi baye baneliseka kukuphawula ukuba ngenye imini elandela ukwaziswa kophando ngokusesikweni, kuye kwacaca ukuba ubonakalisiwe umdla ngokuphendula. Uphando lwakuvalwa ngomhla wama-30 kuJuni yaye iziphumo zakupapashwa kubantu bonke kwamsinyane emva koko.



Iwebhusayithi yabakwaSATI



Usaphethwe umcimbi wokuba le Webhusayithi mayiguqulelwe kuzo zonke iilwimi ezisemthethweni. Kwavunyelwa ekubeni inxalenye yamalungu –ekuku-phela kwecandelo (wakuba sowusebenza lo mbandela) kwakuba kugxilwe kwinkcazelo yamagama angaqhele-kanga nakwiinkumbulo eziguqulelwe kwiilwimi ezingeni-siweyo ngamalungu. Kananjalo kwakubakho icandelo ‘leziBonelelo zokusebenza’ kwindawo eqhelekileyo edwelisa izichazi-magama, njalo-njalo.



Ukucetyiswa ngabanamava



Inkqubo yokucetyiswa ngabanamava ayihambi ngendle-la elindeleke ngayo, kukho ubunzima ekufunyanweni kwamalungu azimisele ukuba ngamavolonti ngokuma-lunga nexesha elifunekayo alinikezelayo kwicala leengcebiso. AbaPhathi bavuma noxa kunjalo ngelithi le yinto efunekayo kuphuhliso lobuchule balo msebenzi, yaye baza kubona ukuba le ngcamango abanakuyivelela ngenye indlela eyahlukileyo na. Enye yezo ndlela ingakukulungiselela ucweyo ngokwamaxesha alandele-lanayo kweli lizwe lonke lethu.



Ukubalelwa kwabamkelekileyo



Ixesha leemviwo ze-NAATI kulo nyaka lakuqaliswa ngomhla wama-23 kuJulayi eJohannesburg naseKapa, ukuvavanyelwa ukutolika kwakubanjwa ngomhla we-12 kuJulayi ePotchefstroom, ngomhla we-14 kuJulayi uvavanyo lwakubanjwa eJohannesburg.








Ubulungu



Okwangoku zisibhozo ii-arhente ezingenele ukuba ngamalungu amanyaniswe neZiko eli, linye iziko lezemfundo elingenele ukuba lilungu elimanyanisiweyo. Bekukho kwakho ingxoxo ngokuba ngamalungu kwaba-fundi: malunga nendlela yokutsalelwa kwabantu ukuba mababe ngamalungu kolu didi, nokuba ubulungu obo bube luncedo kubafundi.



FIT



UMarion Boers uzakumela iZiko eli kwiNkongolo yeFIT eShanghai ngo-Agasti. Amanye amalungu azakube ephaya nawo angaba yinxalenye yabathunywa bakwa-SATI.

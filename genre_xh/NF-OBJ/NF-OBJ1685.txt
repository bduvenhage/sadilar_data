Umhlaza Wamatyhalarha (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)

Ukuzivavanya Amabele (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)
Le ncwadana iqulethe izizathu zokuzivavanya amabele rhoqo. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Uvavanyo Lomhlaza Webele (IiNkonzo)
Abantu basetyhini bayaboniswa ukuba bangawavavanya njani na amabele abo ukuze bafumanise amaqhuba anokuba ngunobangela womhlaza. Ukuba kuthe kwafumaniseka iqhuma, uya kuthunyelwa kuphando oluthe xhaxhe, oluya kuhlanganisa imammogram, ukususwa nokuxilongwa kweqhuma elo ukufumanisa ukuba alinabungozi na (alinamhlaza) okanye linobungozi (linomhlaza) na, ze ke umntu lowo aqeqeshelwe ... &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Iphetshana Elinikezela Iinkcukacha Ngomhlaza Webele (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)
Eli ke liphetshana elinye elinikezela ngeenkcukacha-manani kwanokugqugqisa komhlaza webele kwakunye nemiba ebeka umntu esichengeni sokuhlaselwa lolu hlobo lomhlaza. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Ukuthintela Ukukhulelwa (Ucwangciso-ntsapho) (IiNkonzo)
Le nkonzo ibonelela ngamacebiso malunga neqela leendlela ezikhuselekileyo, ezisebenzayo nezamkelekileyo zokuthintela ukukhulelwa, macebiso lawo abasetyhini, amadoda, umlisela nomthinjana banokuxhamla kuwo ekuthintelweni kokukhulelwa okungadingekiyo. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Ukukhuseleka Kabini (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)
(Uhlobo loxwebhu: pdf; ubukhulu: 92 KB)

Olu xwebhu ke lukhutshelwe oonondaba ngeenjongo zokukhuthaza ukusetyenziswa kweentsilathi kwakunye neendlela ezikhawulezileyo zokuthintela ukukhulelwa ukuze kuthinteleke ezo zisu zingafunwayo, ukosuleleka yintsholongwane kaGawulayo kwakunye nomhlaza wesibeleko. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Uvavanyo Lomhlaza Wesizalo (IiNkonzo)
Umhlaza womlomo wesizalo nowamabele zezona ntlobo zimbini ziqhelekileyo zomhlaza ezifumaneka kubafazi boMzantsi Afrika yaye bonk∆omama basemngciphekweni wokuhlunyelwa zezi ntlobo zomhlaza. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Iphetshana Lenkcazelo Ngomhlaza Wesibeleko Kubasebenzi Becandelo Lezempilo (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)
Eli ke liphetshana Elinikezela Ingcaciso Ngomhlaza Wesibeleko Kubasebenzi Bononophelo Lwempilo. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Khusela Impilo Yakho: iPap Smear (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)
Apha ke sichazela amanina abudala buyiminyaka engaphezu kwamashumi amathathu ngokuba ziintoni na ezi pap smear, nokuba kufuneka zenziwe nini futhi zisenzelwa ntoni na. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Ukukhutshwa Kwesisu (IiNkonzo)
Abantu basetyhini abangafuni kuqhubekeka nokukhulelwa kwabo banokukhetha ukukuphelisa oko kukhulelwa. Ukuqhomfa ungakhange uhlawule nesenti kuyafumaneka kwezinye iikliniki zoMbuso. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

Inkqubo yokungachumi (IiNkonzo)
Inkonzo izama ukunceda omama notata abangafumani bantwana ukuba bakhawule babe nzima. Ingcebiso nolwazi luyanikezelwa mayela neendlela ezahluka-hlukeneyo zonyango ezikhoyo. Imbali yala maqabane iyathatyathwa zize neemvavanyo zenziwe. Kuxhomekeke kwiziphumo, unyango lochumiso luyanikezelwa okanye kucetyiswe ngokuchasene nalo. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Unonophelo Lwempilo Yaxa Sele Uyeke Ukuba Sexesheni Ngenxa Yobudala (IiNkonzo)
Abafazi abayeka ukuya exesheni ngaphambi kwexesha elililo (ngaphambi kobudala obungama- 45 eminyaka) okanye abakwixesha lokuyeka kwaye bahlangabezana neempawu ezibagungqisayo, banakho ukufumana uphononongo koogqirha bomama kwakunye nokuxilongwa. Uvavanyo lwencindi lungenziwa ukuze kubekho izigqibo zonyango. Oomama bangacela ingcebiso ukuze bakwazi ukugqiba ngeyona ndlela yonyango iphucukileyo. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

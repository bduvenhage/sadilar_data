Inkcazelo

Zonke iimoto ezihamba indleleni kawonke-wonke kufuneka amaphepha-mvume azo rhoqo xa kuphela unyaka. Luxanduva lomnini moto ukuqinisekisa ukuba amaphepha-mvume emoto ayavuselelwa.Ukungaphumeleli ukuvuselela amaphepha-mvume emoto kungakhokhelela kwisohlwayo.

Kukhona ixesha lofefe elinga ngentsuku ezingamashumi ambini ananye elinikwa baninimoto ababngekawavuseleli amaphepha-mvume aphelelwe lixesha emoto zabo.Ixesha lofefe libalwa ukusukela ngosuku lokuphelelwa kwamaphepha-mvume.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa I fomu MVL1, lesicelo sephepha-mvume lemoto kwi ofisi yamagosa endlela ekufutshane.
Yiza nencwadi yesazisi kunye nesaziso eso.
Bhatala imali ebekiweyo kwisaziso yokuqulunqwa kwesicelo.

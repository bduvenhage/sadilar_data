Uqeqesho Nophuhliso Kwimidlalo Yeli Lizwe




INKCAZELO:

Eli Sebe liphuhlisa imidlalo yeli ngokuthi iqeqeshe amavolontiya njengeenkokheli zemidlalo yeli lizwe. Imidlalo yeli yayanyaniswa namasiko neenkcubeko yaye futrhi iquka:

* iibeke/uSkununu (umdlalo wokuleqa ibhola),
* iKho-Kho (umdlalo wokubaleka),
* iNtimo/iKgati/uGqaphu (umdlalo wokutsiba intambo),
* iDiketo/iMagave/uPhuca (umdlalo wokuhlanganisa amatye),
* ijukskei (umdlalo wokuleqa ufumane umntu),
* iNcuva/iMorula kunye noMlabalaba (imidlalo yebhodi),
* Iintonga.

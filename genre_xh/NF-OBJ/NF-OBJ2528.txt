Inkcazelo

Iimfuneko zokuphatha nokulawula iqumrhu elingavulelekanga ekuthiwa yi-close corporation  zinobuceducedu bogunyaziso nobunzima obungcono kunobo benkampani. Ngoko ke, amagosa aphezulu esigqeba senkampani angagqiba ekubeni aguqule inkampani ebhalisiweyo ibeyi-CC.

Ukubangaba nalo igama lizakutshintshwa xa inkampani iguqulwa isenziwa i-CC, kufanele kuthunyelwe ikopi yefom evunyiweyo ekuthiwa yi-CK7 nesicelo sokuguqula.

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwi Companies and Intellectual Property Registration Office.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khuphela oluxhwebhu lunezikhewu kwi CK7 ukuze ubeke igama le-CC ukubangaba igama liya kutshintshwa.

Khuphela oluxhwebhu lunezikhewu kwi, CK4  kunye ne-CK1.

Hambisa incwadi echaza igosa elitsha eligunyazisiweyo elimele iinkonzo

Hambisa incwadi evela kumphicothi weencwadi zemali wenkampani ngokweCandelo 27   lomThetho wamaQumrhu angaVulelekanga . Le ncwadi mayichaze ukuba ngubani lo mphicothi weencwadi zemali mtsha.

Hambisa iikopi zeencwadi zezazisi zawo onke amalungu e-CC.

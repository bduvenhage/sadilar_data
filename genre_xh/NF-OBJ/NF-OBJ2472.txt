Inkcazelo

UMhlaba weZolimo oleleyo woMbuso uyaqeshiswa ngeenjongo zolimo ngokunika okanye ngokunganiki ithuba lokukhetha ukuwuthenga, kuxhomekeke kubume balo mhlaba . Izivumelwano zokuqeshisa ezinika ithuba lokuthenga ziyahlaziyeka qho emva kweminyaka emithathu.

ISebe lelizwe leZolimo linoxanduva lokuphembelela ulawulo olufezekisayo nokuhanjiswa komhlaba weZolimo woMbuso owafunyanwa ngokweBhodi yeMboleko yeZolimo ekuthiwa yi-Agricultural Credit Board  land).

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Xa umhlaba wezolimo woMbuso ulungele ukuqeshiswa, iSebe leZolimo liyazisa ngawo ngokuwu-advethayiza ngokwezaziso kwiindawo ezibonakala kakuhle kwingingqi, ezifana nee-ofisi zoomantyi, izikhululo zamapolisa nakumaphephandaba.
Yenza isicelo kulo mntu uxelwe kwisaziso.

Entla

Umthetho osetyenziswayo

Land Administration Act, 1995  
State Land Disposal Act, 1961

UKUTHENGISELA AMAZWE ANGAPHANDLE


Ulwazi Lwenzuzo Yemveliso Ephuma Ngaphandle (IiNkonzo)
ISebe Labongameli liyayithakazelela into yokusasaza ulwazi ngeenkqubo zenzuzo yemvelisa eya ngaphandle, ingakumbi ulwazi lwenzuzo yorhwebo olutsha lwesizwe. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


IZiko loPhuhliso lweMveliso yoMzantsi Afrika Ethengiselwa Amazwe Angaphandle (IiNkonzo)
IZiko leMveliso Ethengiselwa Amazwe Angaphandle lamiselwa kuMzantsi Kapa ukunyusa umgangatho wemveliso ephuma kule ngingqi. Imiswe kwi- Ziko Loshishino loMzantsi Kapa eGeorge, Ziko elo likhuphela imveliso kumazwe angaphandle linikezela abo bathengisela amazwe angaphandle abakhasayo nabasele bekhona ngengcebiso exabisekileyo kwakunye nolwazi lorhwebo. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

Ulwazi ngeziVumelwano eZitsha zoRhwebo (IiNkonzo)
Xa uRhulumente weSizwe ethetha-thethana ngezivumelwano zorhwebo olutsha, aBaphathi beSebe loPhuhliso lezoRhwebo basasaza ulwazi ngezivumelwano kwabo bathengisela amanye amazwe imveliso yeli nakumanye amaqela anomdla. Ukongeza ekunikezeleni umsebenzi obhaliweyo ngezivumelwano zorhwebo, aba baphathi baphinda buqhubae amagumbi okusebenzela. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Iinkcukacha Malunga Nzibonelelo zeSizwe (IiNkonzo)
Abaphathi beSebe loPhuhliso lweMizi-mveliso banceda amashishini ekufikeleleni kulwazi oluphathelelene kwiinkqubo zesizwe zenkxaso. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

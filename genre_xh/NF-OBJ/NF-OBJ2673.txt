Inkcazelo

Olu ncedo lulungiselelwe abantu abavelisa iimoto, abenza iimoto,abathenga bathengise iimoto. Inanai lokurhweba ngeemoto linika imvume kwababantu babaliweyo yokuba baqhube endleleni kawonke-wonke iimoto ezingabhaliswanga.

Nani lokurhweba lifunelwa okukuba:

abavelisi bemoto, bazise bathengise batshintshe okanye balungise iimoto
abathuthi bemoto bahambise iimoto
abarhwebi ngemoto ukuze bakwazi ukuhambisa iimoto
nabathenga iimoto kwamanye amazwe ukuze bakwazi ukuzihambisa.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa ifomu MTN 1 e ofisini yamagosa endlela ekufuphi nawe.
Bonakalisa ukuba kutheni ufuna amanani okurhweba nokuba kutheni ufuna elo nana olifunayo.
Thumela lamaxwebhu alandelayo:

ikopi yencwadi yesazisi okanye uxwebhu lelungelo lokumela xa umele inkampani
isatifiketi sokubhaliswa kweshishini

Bhatala imali efunekayo.

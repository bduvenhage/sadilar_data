Inkcazelo

Umntu ongummi waseMzantsi Afrika ngokomnombo nguye nabanina ozalelwe ngaphandle koMzantsi Afrika ngabazali apho okungenani omnye wabazali engummi waseMzantsi Afrika. Ukuzalwa komntwana kufuneka kubhaliswe ngokuhambisana noMthetho wokuBhaliswa kokuZalwa nokuBhubha .

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa ifom ekuthiwa yi BI-24.
Umzali ongowaseMzantsi Afrika makagcwalise ifom ekuthiwa yi BI-529.
Kwimeko yokwamkelwa komntwana ngumntu ongamzelanga ibengowakhe , thumela umyalelo wokwamkelwa komntwana ngumntu ongamzelanga ibengowakhe 
Thumela ikopi yesi satifiketi sokuzalwa selizwe langaphandle.
Thumela iikopi zezatifiketi zomtshato zomenzi-sicelo nezomzali 
Ukubangaba umenzi-sicelo wazalelwa ngaphandle komtshato, bobabini abazali mabasayine ifom yokubhaliswa kokuzalwa ethi BI-24.
Thumela ubungqina ukubangaba umzali waseMzantsi Afrika ufumene ubumi belizwe langaphandle.
Thumela ubungqina bokuzalwa kwalo mzali waseMzantsi Afrika.

Entla

Inkcazelo

Umntu olahlekelwe okanye obelwe ipaspoti ebenayo angenza isicelo sokunikwa entsha.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Izinto eziyimfuneko ukuze ufumane enye ipaspoti endaweni yaleyo ilahlekileyo okanye ibiweyo :

khupha incwadi yesazisi okanye isatifiketi sokuzalwa esingafinyezwanga ukuze wenze isicelo
gcwalisa ifom yesicelo ekuthiwa yi BI-73 kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya. Yenza isicelo kwiNdlu yoZakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiZiko eliMele uMzantsi Afrika ukubangaba ukwilizwe langaphandle
nyathelisa iminwe  ukuze ifakwe kuvimba woBhaliso lweNani neeNkcukacha zaBantu
khupha iifoto ezimbini ezilingana kwipaspoti.

Izinto eziyimfuneko ukuze ufumane enye ipaspoti yomntwana endaweni yaleyo ilahlekileyo okanye ibiweyo:

gcwalisa ifom yesicelo ekuthiwa yi BI-73 kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya. Yenza isicelo kwiNdlu yoZakuzo yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye kwiZiko eliMele uMzantsi Afrika ukubangaba ukwilizwe langaphandle
abazali okanye abalondolozi basemthethweni kufuneka bakhuphe ikopi yesatifiketi somntwana esingafinyezwanga, ndawonye neencwadi zabo zezazisi
khupha iifoto zomntwana ezimbini ezilingana kwipaspoti
abantwana abaneminyaka yobudala engaphantsi kwe 16 abayinyatheliswa iminwe .

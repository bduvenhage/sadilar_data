Inkcazelo

Le nkonzo ivumela umntu, iziko okanye umbutho ukuba useke inkqubo yokunxibelelana ne-South African Revenue Service  ngobuxhakaxhaka bomatshini ngokonxibelelwano lobuchwepheshe obuchaziweyo. INkqubo eNtsha yeRhafu yeNgeniso-mali iqingqelwe ukunciphisa imveliso yeencwadi ngezandla nangokwamaphepha aphathekayo, kunye nokufaka uninzi lweenkqubo ezisetyenzwa ngezandla kubuxhakaxhaka bomatshini, kwaye kwangaxeshanye kuqinisekiswe ukuba abarhafi baphathwa ngokulinganayo. Kuveliswe iinkqubo ezahlukeneyo zokunxibelelana ngobuxhakaxhaka bomatshini.

I-Interface User Guide  ifumaneka kwi-SARS Online kwaye ijoliswe ekubeni iphendule uninzi lwemibuzo nezicelo zengcaciso.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Bhalisela ukubangugxa wonxibelelwano ngobuxhakaxhaka bomatshini ngokugcwalisa nokuthumela ifom yobhaliso yobuxhakaxhaka bomatshini, enenombolo ethi INF001 kwi: SARS Interface Administration, P.O. Box 402, Pretoria 0001 okanye nge e-mail kwi-adresi ethi: its.interfaces@sars.gov.za. Izicelo zokongezelwa ixesha lokubuyisa iifom zerhafu yengeniso-mali zingenziwa ngokomatshini ngaphandle kwemfuneko yokubhalisa.
Gcina ezi nkcukacha zilandelayo zinikwe yi-SARS emva kobhaliso:

igama lokungena eliyimfihlo ukuze ufikelele kubuxhakaxhaka be-SARS
uphawu lokwazisa inkqubo elinikwe umbutho yi-SARS
uphawu lokwazisa umsebenzisi kunye negama eliyimfihlo lokungena kwifayili yomatshini ye-SARS apho kuyakukhutshelwa khona ifayili yenkcazelo
i-adresi yonxibelelwano lobuxhakaxhaka okanye iinombolo zokucofa, kwimeko yoNxibelelwano olusebenzisa iMfonomfono ekuthiwa yi-Public Switched Telephone Network  kunye nonxibelelwano nge-Integrated Services Digital Network .

Fumana iinkqubo ezifakwa kwikhompiyutha zokutshintshela inkcazelo kwiimpawu ezithile  kunye nemigaqo yokufaka inkqubo esuka kwi-SARS. Ngokwesiqhelo i-SARS iyazizisa kulo mbutho ezi nkqubo zifakwa kwikhompiyutha.
Faka inkqubo engena kwikhompiyutha yokutshintshela inkcazelo kwiimpawu ezithile.
Thumela iifayili zovavanyo lwenkcazelo kwi-SARS, kwaye ukhuphe iifayili zempendulo yovavanyo kwi-SARS.
Vuma ukuba uzamkele iifayili zovavanyo lwenkcazelo kuMlawuli woBuxhakaxhaka booMatshini be SARS kwaye usebenzise unxibelelwano lobuxhakaxhaka bomatshini ngokuthumela iifayile zenkcazelo yokwenyani.
Nxulumana noMlawuli woBuxhakaxhaka boomatshini be-SARS xa kuthe kwakho iifayile zenkcazelo ezonakalisekileyo, iifayili zenkcazelo ezilahlekileyo okanye iifayili zenkcazelo eziphindaphindeneyo.

Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo

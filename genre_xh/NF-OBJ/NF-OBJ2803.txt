Inkcazelo

Esi sicelo kufuneka sithiwe thaca kwi-South African Revenue of Revenue  kwiimeko zokurhoxa, ukurizayina, intlawulo yolwabiwo eyintsalela, iinzuzo ezingabangwanga, iintlawulo zomhlathi  wenkcazelo 'yengeniso-mali epheleleyo' njengoko kuchaziwe kuMthetho weRhafu yeNgeniso-mali, wango 1962  okanye ukutshintshelwa kwenzuzo yelungu ukusuka kwingxowa-mali yepenshele okanye yokubonelela, okanye kwimeko apho ingxowa-mali yepenshele okanye yokubonelela ivalwa.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khupha iFom B kwi-website uyigcwalise ngokupheleleyo, uze ke uyihambise phambi kokuba uhlawule isixa sesambuku kwilungu lengxowa-mali.
Khupha inombolo ye-ID yelungu okanye olunye uhlobo lokuzazisa . Kungcono ukusebenzisa inombolo ye-ID.
Nika i-adresi yelungu yokuhlala neyokuposa.
Qulungqa ingeniso-mali yelungu ngokonyaka nangokuchaneke kangangoko kukwazekayo. Akumelanga ibengaphantsi komgangatho ophakathi  yentlawulo yomvuzo welungu  kunyaka wovavanyo omiyo nongaphambili.
Nawuphina umyalelo wokuxhuzula imali  okhutshwe yi-SARS kufuneka uthotyelwe.

Eyona ndlela ilula kakhulu yokwenza isicelo sesikhokelo serhafu kukusifaka nge- eFiling. Isicelo sakho sesikhokelo serhafu esenziwe ngale nkonzo sidla ngokuvunywa kwisithuba seeyure ezingama 48.

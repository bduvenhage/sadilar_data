Inkcazelo
Abenzi-zicelo abanqwenela ukubhalisa basengafuneka bazalisekise imiqathango ethile phambi kobhaliso okanye emva kobhaliso. Oku kukwabandakanya imiqathango eboniswe kulungiso lomgaqo-siseko waMaqumrhu.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Isicelo sakho sobhaliso kufuneka siqulathe le nkcazelo ilandelayo:
o iinkcukacha zequmrhu lokukhusela umlilo elingumgqatswa o iinkcukacha zomntu oqalisa inyathelo lobhaliso lwequmrhu lokukhusela umlilo elingumgqatswa o isibhengezo sokuba akukho mnikazi okhethwe ngabom kubandakanyo kwiintlanganiso neengxoxo malunga nokusekwa kweli qumrhu lokukhusela umlilo o iinkcukacha zommandla elinegunya lokusebenza kuwo eli qumrhu lokukhusela umlilo lingumgqatswa o isibhengezo sokuba alikho elinye iqumrhu lokukhusela umlilo elijongiweyo okanye elikhoyo ngaphakathi kulo mmandla wequmrhu lokukhusela umlilo elingumgqatswa o isibhengezo esenziwe ngumntu oqalisa inyathelo lokusekwa kwequmrhu lokukhusela umlilo o isiphakamiso esenziwe ngummeli ofanelekileyo worhulumente wendawo yabahlali o isiphakamiso esenziwe ngummeli wengingqi wesebe lezamanzi namahlathi o isibhengezo esenziwe ngumqondisi oyintloko wengingqi weSebe lezaManzi naMahlathi.

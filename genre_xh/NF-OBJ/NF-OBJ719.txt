Ukufaka Isicelo Sesiqinisekiso Somtshato




INKCAZELO:

Xa utshata, unikwa isiqinisekiso somtshato esingahlawulelwayo. Ukuba ufuna ukufumana esinye isiqinisekiso somtshato ngosuku olusezayo, kufuneka:
* Kufuneka ugcwalise iphetshana lesicelo u-BI-130
* Ulifake kwi-ofisi ekufutshane nawe yeSebe Lemicimbi yeKhaya
* Unikezele intlawulo.
Isiqinisekiso esifinyeziweyo siza kunikezelwa kwangaloo mhla wesicelo. Isicelo sesiqinisekiso esipheleleyo sithatha phakathi kweeveki ezintandathu nezisibhozo ukuphunyezwa.

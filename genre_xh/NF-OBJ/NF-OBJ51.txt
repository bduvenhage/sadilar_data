Inkcazelo
Umntu ongummi waseMzantsi Afrika ngokomnombo nguye nabanina ozalelwe ngaphandle koMzantsi Afrika ngabazali apho okungenani omnye wabazali engummi waseMzantsi Afrika. Ukuzalwa komntwana kufuneka kubhaliswe ngokuhambisana noMthetho wokuBhaliswa kokuZalwa nokuBhubha (Births and Deaths Registration Act, 1992).

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Thumela ikopi yesi satifiketi sokuzalwa selizwe langaphandle.
Thumela ubungqina ukubangaba umzali waseMzantsi Afrika ufumene ubumi belizwe langaphandle.
Thumela ubungqina bokuzalwa kwalo mzali waseMzantsi Afrika.

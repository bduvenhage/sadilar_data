Inkcazelo
Xa idilesi yomntu itshintshile emva kokubhaliswa kwi-SARS njengomrhafi, makanike i-SARS iinkcukacha zedilesi entsha kwisithuba seentsuku ezingama- 60 ukusukela kumhla wokutshintsha.
Nayo iNcwadi yokuBuyisa yeRhafu yeNgeniso (Income Tax Return) esiwa kwi-SARS qho ngonyaka iya kulungiselela ukuxelwa koguquko lwedilesi, iinkcukacha zebhanki kunye nezinye iinkcukacha zobuqu. Isininzi sezaziso neencwadi zokubuyisa ze-SARS ziyawenza amalungiselelo okuxelwa kotshintsho lweenkcukacha zedilesi. Ezo nguqu ziya kulungiswa kwinkqubo ebandakanyekayo ye-SARS xa isaziso okanye incwadi yokubuyisa zizokufakwa.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Qinisekisa ukuba inkcazelo ngobuqu bakho njengoko ibonisiwe kwizaziso okanye kwiincwadi zokubuyisa ezamkelwa yi-SARS, ichanekile. Ukubangaba inkcazelo iboniswe ngokungachanekanga, phawula inkcazelo echanekileyo kwisaziso okanye kwincwadi yokubuyisa ebandakanyekayo phambi kokuba uyithumele kwi-SARS.
Faka iinguqu kwiinkcukacha zobuqu okanye kwiidilesi ngezi ndlela zilandelayo:
o thumela isicelo sokwenza iinguqu kwi-ofisi yesebe le-SARS ekufuphi kuwe o yenza izicelo ngokutsalela umnxeba kwiZiko lokuTsalela uMnxeba le-SARS ebandakanyekayo o thumela ifeksi kumaZiko okuTsalela uMnxeba eSARS ebandakanyekayo okanye kwi-ofisi yesebe le-SARS ekufuphi kuwe.

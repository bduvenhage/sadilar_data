Inkcazelo
Inkcazelo ephathelelene nobhaliso lwepropati engashukumiyo, efana nale ilandelayo:
o amagama, iinkcukacha zokuzazisa kunye nobume bomtshato babanikazi ababhalisileyo o amanani emiyalelo yenkundla yokuthintela izenzo ezithile efakelwe abantu kunye nepropati o iinkcukacha zokutshintshelwa kwepropati, kubandakanywa imihla yobhaliso kunye namaxabiso okuthenga o iinkcukacha zobambiso ngepropati njengesetyhorithi yemali-mboleko,  kubandakanywa amagama wabantu ababambise ngeepropati njengesetyhorithi yemali-mboleko kunye neemali eziyinkunzi

Hlawula imali yokubhalisa. Imiyalelo yebhanki kunye neenkcukacha ziyabonakala kwi-website.

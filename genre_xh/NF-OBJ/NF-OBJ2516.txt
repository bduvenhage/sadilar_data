Inkcazelo

Iqumrhu elingavulelekanga, ekuthiwa yi-close corporation  liqumrhu loshishino elinabanikazi abangamalungu aphakathi kwesinye nama- 10. Izabelo zeli qumrhu azinantengiso kuluntu. Nangona nje i-CC ifuneka ibenegosa elimele iinkonzo, akukho mfuneko yezitetimenti eziphicothiweyo.

Nangona nje uMthetho weeNkampani , umisela iintlanganiso ezisisinyanzelo kwiinkampani, ngakumbi malunga neentlanganiso zoluntu, akukho mfuneko malunga noku kwii-CC. Iintlanganiso zidla ngokubanjwa phakathi kwamalungu ngeloo xesha kukho imfuneko ethile.

Akukho mfuneko yokuba amalungu e-CC ewonke adlale indima enentshukumo ekuqhutyweni kweshishini. Kodwa ke, kuninzi lwee-CC, amalungu akwangoomanejala beshishini.
Amanqaku abalulekileyo aphathelelene nokuzaliswa kwefom, adweliswe kwiphepha elingemva loku- 1 lefom ekuthiwa yi-CK1.

Ukuze ufumane inkcazelo ebanzi, yiya kwi Companies and Intellectual Property Registration Office.

Fumana ifom yesicelo, CK1.
Tayipa ngococeko ifom ekuthiwa yi-CK1 okanye uyizalise nge-inki emnyama, usebenzise oonobumba abangadityaniswanga.
Khupha iikopi eziqinisekisiweyo zeencwadi zezazisi zamalungu abandakanyekayo.
Beka igama leli qumrhu lingavulelekanga, CK7.
Fumana incwadi yemvume esuka kwiGosa eliMele iiNkonzo.

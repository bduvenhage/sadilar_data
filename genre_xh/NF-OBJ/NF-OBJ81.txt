Inkcazelo
lo mntwana wazalelwa ngaphandle komtshato kwaye umama wakhe utshate omnye umntu ongenguye uyise ozele lo mntwana umtshato notata womntwana uchithiwe ngokoqhawulo lomtshato (ngokwedivosi) okanye ngenxa yokubhubha, kwaye umama uphinde watshata njengomhlolokazi okanye umntu oqhawule umtshato, umama wakhe ubuyele kwifani yaphambi komtshato okanye kwifani yomtshato wangaphambili ukuzalwa komntwana owazalelwa ngaphandle komtshato kubhaliswe phantsi kwefani yotata wakhe omzeleyo.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Veza imvume ebhaliweyo evela kulo myeni umntwana azakusebenzisa ifani yakhe.
Ukuze kuguqulwe (apho kubandakanyeka khona), iyafuneka imvume ebhaliweyo evela kubazali bobabini, ndawonye nezizathu ezaneleyo, ezibhaliweyo.
Iyafuneka imvume ebhaliweyo yotata ozele lo mntwana, ngaphandle kokuba isuselwe ecaleni yinkundla enamandla negunya lokwenza njalo.

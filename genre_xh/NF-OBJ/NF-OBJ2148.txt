Small Business Development Agency (SEDA) Website (Qhagamshela) (ifumaneka ngesiNgesi kuphela)
The Small Enterprise Development Agency (seda) is the Department of Trade and Industry's agency for supporting small business in South Africa. It contains information on starting and running your own business, including finance, management, tendering, BEE and more. There is also a collection of useful factsheets to download. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

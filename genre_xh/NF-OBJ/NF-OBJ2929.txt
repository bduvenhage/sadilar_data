Inkcazelo
Isikhululo sovavanyo lwemoto liziko elivavanya iimoto likhuphe isatifiketi sokulungela ukuhamba endleleni.Kukhona izikhululo zovavanyo lwemoto ezizimeleyo nezikarhulumente. Zonke izikhululo zovavanyo lwemoto kufuneka zibhaliswe, zinikwe izinga lokusebenza ngaphambi kokuba zikhuphe isatifiketi sokulungela ukuhamba endlelen. Lisakufumana isicelo iCandelo lezoThutho lizakuthumela umhloli ayokujongisisa ukuba isikhululo sovavanyo abone ukuba siyilandele imithetho yobugcisa obubeka isikhululo emgangathweni wobhaliso obekwe yingqokelela yemithetho Yolawulo loMgangatho yase Mzantsi Afrika (SABS).
Ukuphumelelangobugcisa kubhekiselele ezintweni zokusebenza zesikhululo sovavanyo noqeqesho abalugqibileyo abavavanyi bemoto.
Izikhululo zovavanyo ezibhalisiweyo zingavavanya kuphela iimoto ngokomgangatho wesikhululo eso.
Ukubhalisa isikhululo esizimeleyo sovavanyo lwemoto, dibana ne ofisi lamagosa endlela ekufutshane ufumane ulwazi oluphangalele.

Inkcazelo
Ikhaya labantwana yindawo ebhalisiweyo yokunakekela abantwana. Amakhaya wabantwana angabanga imali yoncediso oluyisabhsidi yenyanga kurhulumente ukuze afezekise iindleko zawo eziqhubekayo. Imali yesabhsidi ekhutshwayo ayifani kwaye isekelwe kwiindleko eziqhubekayo zeli khaya kunye nenani labantwana abahlala kulo.
Ngamakhaya wabantwana abhalisiweyo.

Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo
Inkqubo yesicelo iyakuthabatha ngaphezulu kweenyanga ezintathu ukuze isicelo siqhubekiswe.
Esi sigqibo sisekelwe ekufumanekeni kwezinto eziyimfuneko kunye nemfuneko yale nkonzo.
Isebe likhupha ezi mali zoncediso oluyisabhsidi qho ngeenyanga ezintathu, kuxhomekeke kuqwalaselo ngokutsha lwemisebenzi yeli khaya labantwana.

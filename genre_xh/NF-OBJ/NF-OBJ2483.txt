Inkcazelo

Isiza apho impahla yofuzo  iqokelelwa, ihlolwe, ihlelwe, ipakwe, ifakwe iileyibhile, igcinwe yaye ithengiswe khona, singabhaliswa njengeziko xa:

amalungiselelo amiselweyo efumaneka kwisiza esinjalo
imisebenzi yobuchwepheshe kwisiza esinjalo, efana nonakekelo lwezilwanyana ezigcinwe apho, ukuqokelela, ukuhlola, ukuhlela, ukupakisha, ukwenza iileyibhile nogcino lwempahla yofuzo, ilawulwa nokongamelwa ngumntu owanelisa iimfuno ezimiselweyo
isiza sisanelisa ezinye iimfuno ezingamiselwa.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Khupha ifomu yesicelo kwi-inthanethi uze uyizalise ngeekopi ezintathu. Ifomu kananjalo ingacelwa ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement .
Izicelo ezingazaliswanga ngokupheleleyo ziya kuphindiswa kumenzi wesicelo yaye akukho xanduva luya kwamkelwa malunga nakuphi na ukuphazamiseka okunokwenzeka.
Qhoboshela amaxwebhu alandelayo kwifomu:

iikopi ezimbini zesicwangciso sesiza esichaphazelekayo nezicwangciso zomhlaba ngokunzulu
intlawulo emiselweyo yesicelo ngetsheki ehlawulwa ku-: The Director-General: Agriculture.

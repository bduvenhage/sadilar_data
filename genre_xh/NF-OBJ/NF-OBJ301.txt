UKUHLAWULWA KWERENTE


Ukusonjululwa Kwempikiswano Phakathi Kwabahlali Abaqeshileyo Kunye Nabanini Bezakhiwo (IiNkonzo)
iNkundla Ejongene Nezindlu Eziqeshisayo inikezela ngeenkonzo ezisimahla kubaqeshi bezindlu kunye nabaqeshisi kwiPhondo lonke leNtshona Koloni. Ukuxoxwa kwamatyala kunye nolamlo lubanjwa ngethutyana nje elifutshane emva kokwenziwa kwesikhalazo. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo Yabantu Abahlala Kwiindawo Abaziqeshileyo (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)
Ezi ziinkcukacha ngamalungelo noxanduva lwabantu abahlala kwiindaw abaziqeshileyo nabaqeshe iindawo zokuhlala kubanini-mihlaba okanye kwabo basebenzela abo banini-mihlaba. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;


Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo Ngabanini-mihlaba (IiNkcukacha Zoluntu)
Ezi ke ziinkcukacha ngamalungelo noxanduva lwabanini-mihlaba abaqeshisa ngezakhiwo ukuze kuhlale abanye abantu. &nbsp;&gt;&nbsp;

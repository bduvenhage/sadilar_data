Inkcazelo
Le nkonzo yeyabantu abadala abafuna ukukhathalelwa lonke ixesha, ekhayeni labadala.
Ngubani ongafaka isicelo?
Ngabantu abadala abafuna inkathalo ngalo lonke ixesha.
Ngamadoda aminyaka ingamashumi amathandathu nesihlanu nangaphezulu, kunye nabafazi abaminyaka ingamashumi amathandathu nangaphezulu.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Ufakwa kuvavanyo lokuhlola ukuba ufanelwe ukufumana inkxaso mali nolwamkeleko.
Xa Siphumelele isicelo sakho, kufuneka utyikitye isivumelwano phakathi kwakho nekhaya labadala.

Ukuqwalasela isicelo sakho kungathabatha intsuku ezingamashumi amathathu nesihlanu. Ixesha elimisiweyo lixhomekeke kwinani labantu abalindileyo.

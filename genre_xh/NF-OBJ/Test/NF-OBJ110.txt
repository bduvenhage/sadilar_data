Inkcazelo
NgokoMthetho wokuLawulwa koRhwebo lwaMazwe eHlabathi (International Trade Administration Act, Act 71 of 2002), uMphathiswa wezoRhwebo naMashishini, naninina xa ebona ukuba kuyimfuneko okanye kuluncedo kwiminqweno neemfuno zoluntu, angamisela ngesaziso kwiGazethi kaRhulumente, ukuba kungabikho zimpahla nezinto zodidi okanye zohlobo olungqalileyo ezingangeniswa eMzantsi Afrika; okanye ezingangeniswa eMzantsi Afrika ngaphandle kwaphantsi kwegunya lepemethe kunye nangokuhambisana nemiqathango exelwe kwipemethe ekhutshwe nguye okanye ngumntu ogunyaziswe nguye.
Izicelo zeepemethe zokungeniswa kwempahla nezinto, eziphantsi kwamanyathelo olawulo lokungeniswa kwempahla nezinto elizweni, kufuneka zenziwe kwifom emiselwe oku.

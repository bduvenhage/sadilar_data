Inkcazelo
Okwangoku, kufumaneka ezi fom zerhafu zilandelayo:
Abarhafi banikwa ithuba lokufumana, ukugcwalisa, ukuthumela nokuhlawula irhafu ngeendlela zokuhlawula ezithembekileyo nezikhuselekileyo, ngamaxesha onke ngemini.
Entla
Inkqubo emayilandelwe
Khetha iqhosha lokubhalisa elingasekhohlo, uze ukhethe uhlobo lobhaliso olubandakanyeka kuwe.
Uphawu olusisitshixo sokuvula kunye negama lokungena eliyimfihlo yakho ziyakukhutshwa kuphela xa umrhafi okanye ummeli womrhafi, eqondiwe ukuba nguye nyhani na, ngokuhambisana namanyathelo ogqaliselo losayino lwe-website.
Akuba umrhafi eyigcwalisile ifom yerhafu, inkcazelo efakwe kwifom iphononongwa ngokugqibeleleyo ukuze kuqinisekwe ukuba ifanelekile. Lonke ubalo lwenziwa yile nkqubo yomatshini ukuze kususwe naliphina ithuba lokufakeka kwenkcazelo engachanekanga.
Umrhafi angakhetha ukuhlawula xa ebuyisa ifom yerhafu kwaye angayenza loo ntlawulo naninina phambi komhla ekufanele ingene ngawo.
Nangona nje le nkqubo ivakala ngathi inzima, eneneni, xa uyisebenzisa, ilula kwaye iyakhawuleza. Uncedo ulufumana nje ngokutsalela umnxeba kwiZiko lokuTsalela uMnxeba nakwiCandelo leNkxaso elinesitafu esaneleyo ukuze ukhokelwe kule nkqubo.

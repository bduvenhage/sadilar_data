Inkcazelo

Oku kukwazisa iCandelo lezoThutho ngotshintsho lwe dilesi yomnini moto. Bonke abanini moto kufuneka baqinisekise ukuba amadilesi wabo athathiwe kubhaliso lwemoto ngachanekileyo.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Yiya kwi ofisi yamagosa endlela ekufuphi nawe.
Gcwalisa uthumele ifomu lesaziso.
Yiza nala maxwebhu alandelayo:

ikopi yencwadi yesazisi
ubungqina bendawo yokuhlala entsha.

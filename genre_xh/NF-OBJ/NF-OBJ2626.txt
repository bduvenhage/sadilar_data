Inkcazelo

Le nkonzo isebenziseka kumntu welizwe langaphandle ozimeleyo ngokwemali. Izicelo zingenziwa kuyo nayiphina i-ofisi yezeKhaya okanye kwiziko elimele uMzantsi Afrika ukubangaba wenza isicelo ukwilizwe langaphandle.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Gcwalisa ifom yesicelo ekuthiwa yi BI-947 kunye ne BI-29.
Hambisa uqinisekiso olwenziwe ngumgcini nomhloli wee-akhawunti zemali olilungu lequmrhu labagcini nabahloli bee-akhawunti zemali , lokuba unemali eyinzuzo eyeyakho nqo ebubuncinane besiseko esimiselweyo esixabisa iR7,5 million, kwaye uyihlawule imali ebekiweyo kwiSebe lezeKhaya eyi R75 000.

Inkcazelo

Abavoti ababhalisileyo bangaqondisisa ukuba ingaba babhaliswe ngokuchanekileyo na kuluhlu lwamagama wabavoti. Ukubangaba igama lakho alikho kuluhlu lwamagama wabavoti, uyakucetyiswa ukuba utyelele i-ofisi ekwindawo ohlala kuyo yeKomishoni eZimeleyo yoNyulo, ekuthiwa yi-Independent Electoral Commission , ukuze uphinde ubhalise.

Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Nxulumana ne-IEC ngexesha leeyure zokusebenza kwinombolo ethi 0800 11 8000  kwaye ubanike inombolo yakho yencwadi yesazisi .
Thumela umyalezo ngeselulafowuni  kwaye ufake inombolo yakho ye-ID, kwinombolo ethi 32810.
Kungenjalo, tyelela i-IEC website ukuze uqondisise kwi-online.

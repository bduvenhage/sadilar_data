Inkcazelo

Abavoti ababhalisileyo abatshintshe iindawo zokuhlala kufuneka baphinde babhalise kwizithili zabo ezitsha zokuvota.Oku kuyakukunceda ekubeni ukwazi ukuvotela kwisithili sokuvota ohlala kuso. Ungaphinda ubhalise kwi-ofisi ekwindawo ohlala kuyo yeKomishoni eZimeleyo yoNyulo, ekuthiwa yi-Independent Electoral Commission , okanye xa kuvezwe amathuba apapashwe yi-IEC wokuba abavoti babhalise.



Entla

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

Nxulumana ne-IEC kwinombolo yomnxeba engahlawulelwayo ethi 0800 11 8000 kwaye ubanike i-adresi yakho entsha yendawo ohlala kuyo.
Iyakuthi ke i-IEC ikolathise i-ofisi echanekileyo yokubhalisa.
Xa ukwi-ofisi yobhaliso, veza incwadi yesazisi enomqukumbelo .
Gcwalisa ifom yesicelo oyinikwe kwi-ofisi yobhaliso.

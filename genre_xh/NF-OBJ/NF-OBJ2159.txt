Iingcebiso Ngezifundo Ezinokuthatyathwa




INKCAZELO:

Esi Sikhundla Sonxibelelwano Solutsha lweMsomsobomvu siqulethe iinkcukacha ngezifundo ezahlukeneyo ezinokuthi zithatyathwe kwaneemfuno zokungena kwisifundo ngasinye. Ulwazi olulapho luquka:
* ingcaciso ngezo zifundo
* iimpawu zomntu onokuthabatha ezo zifundo
* inqanaba lokufunda olufunekayo (kuquka nokuzikhethela izifundo ozithandayo)
* izifundo zeziko lemfundo ephakamileyo ezifunwayo
* abaqeshi abanokuba nomdla kwabo bafundi
* imithombo yolwazi enokuba luncedo ngezo zifundo umntu azikhethileyo
* izifundo ezisonde kwezo umntu azikhethileyo
* amathuba oncedo ngenkxaso-mali yokufunda.

Umsobomvu uvelise incwadana ebizwa ngokuba yi- "Umsobomvu uvelise incwadana ebizwa ngokuba yi- "<a href="http://www.youthportal.org.za/ur/Education%20and%20Training_Career%20Planning_Final.pdf" target="_blank>Career Planning and Development: A Guide for Young People</a>" ukukunceda ufikelele kwizigqibo ngezifundo onomdla kuzo.
Esi sikhundla sonxibelelwano siqulethe ulwazi ngokuba ungawukhangela njani na umsebenzi.


IMIYALELO:

Ukuze ukwazi ukuphendla esi sikhundla solwazi sekhompyutha, ndwendwela le dilesiwww.youthportal.org.za.Usenakho nokutsalela umnxeba iZiko Leminxeba leYouth Connect kule nombolo: 08600 YOUTH (08600 96884).

Ubuxhaka-xhaka Bobugcisa Kumakhosikazi Akushishino




INKCAZELO:

Inkqubo Yokunikezelwa Kobuxhaka-xhaka Bobugcisa Kumakhosikazi Akushishino ingumvuka wentsebenziswano phakathi kweSebe Leentengiso Noshishino, iSebe Lezimbiwa Namandla kwakunye neCSIR ngonyaka ka-1998 ngeenjongo zokunceda ekuphakanyisweni kwamakhosikazi akushishino. Ukugxininisa kwale nkqubo ekusetyenzisweni kwenzululwazi nobuxhaka-xhaka bobugcisa ukuze kuzuzwe ukukhula koshishino kumashishini akhokelwa ngamakhosikazi, ingakumbi amashishini amancinci, aphakathi kwanalawo makhulu.

IINTSHUKUMO


Iintshukumo ziya kuquka phakathi kwezinye:
* ukuchongwa nokwaziwa kweemfuno zamakhosikazi akushishino kubuxhaka-xhaka bobugcisa kumacandelo oshishino asele echongiwe
* ukumelana neemfuno esele zichongiwe ngokungenelela ngobuxhaka-xhaka bobugcisa ngendlela eyodwa kwiphulo elithile
* ukubekwa kweliso ekusebenzisaneni kwamakhosikazi akushishino namaqumrhu anikezela ngeenkonzo zobuxhaka-xhaka bobugcisa ukuchongwa nokudalwa kwamacandelo kwanamathuba oshishino kusenzelwa abo bayingxenye yenkqubo yeTWIB
* ukunakanwa nokubhiyozelwa kwempumelelo yamakhosikazi kumacandelo ahlukeneyo oshishino
* ukuchongwa nokunikezelwa kwenkxaso kubafundi kwicandelo leNzululwazi nobuxhaka-xhaka bobugcisa
* ukusekwa nokugcinwa kwesikhundla solwazi esiqulethe ulwazi oluya kuba lulutho kwiintshukumo zeTWIB
* ukusekwa nokugcinwa kwewebsite yeTWIB
* ukuvezwa kwamakhosikazi aseMzantsi Afrika nokwaziswa kwawo kwiintshukumo zenzululwazi nobuxhaka-xhaka bobugcisa behlabathi ukubonakaliswa kwanokuthatyathwa kwexesha kusandiswa ubuxhaka-xhaka bobugcisa
* ukufukanywa kwamashishini asakhulayo.


UQEQESHO

I-TWIB ikwabonelela ngezifundo zoqeqesho kumacandelo athile. Okwangoku isezezi izifundo ekusanikezelwa ngazo:
* Amathuba Kumakhosikazi Kwanabanini Bamashishini Ezimbiwa
* Amakhosikazi Ashishina Ngokuloba Kwanokulungiselelwa Intengiso Kwentlanzi
* Ukwaziswa Kweekhompyutha
* Ubuxhaka-xhaka Bolwazi Nonxibelelwano Kushishino
* Ushishino Ngobuxhaka-xhaka Beekhompyutha


Amabhaso eTWIB

I-TWIB ikwabamba umnyhadala wamabhaso minyaka le ukunakana nokuvuza amakhosikazi asebenzise ngempumelelo ubugcisa obububo ukuphucula intsebenzo yamashishini awo. Umnyhadala Wamabhaso eTWIB ka-2004 uya kubanjwa ngomhla wethoba apha kweyeThupha ku-2004.


Ophumelele ngo-2004 ibingu-Isabelle Rorke, umseki we-Anamazing Workshop - eyinkampani enobuchule bokwenza abalinganiswa abazizilwanyana neyilayo.

Ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ngokhuphiswano luka-2005, tyelela iwebsite ye-Technology for Women in Business.

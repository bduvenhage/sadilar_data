Inkcazelo
Ipaspoti yongxamiseko yincwadi efuneka ngokukhawuleza xa ujonge ukuhambela kumazwe angabamelwane angafuni ii-visa zokungena. Ipaspoti yongxamiseko ingasetyenziselwa kwanokubuyela eMzantsi Afrika ngokungxamisekileyo. Isebenziseka iinyanga ezilithoba ukusukela kumhla wokukhutshwa kwayo, kodwa isetyenziswa kuhambo olunye kuphela, ukuya kwilizwe elibandakanyekayo nokubuyela eMzantsi Afrika.

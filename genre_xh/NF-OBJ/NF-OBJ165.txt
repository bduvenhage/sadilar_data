Inkcazelo
Nawuphi na umntu ojonge ukunika inkonzo yosasazo lorhwebo kufuneka ahambisane noluhlu lweenkqubo njengoko kuchaziwe kwiSimemo seZicelo ekuthiwa yi-Invitation to Apply (ITA), seLayisensi yoRhwebo yoSasazo lweZandi, esipapashwa ngokusemthethweni kwiGazethi kaRhulumente. Olu luhlu lweenkqubo ludla ngokuba namacandelo amathandathu, angala alandelayo:
IiMeko neMiqathango ngokubanzi: inkqubo emayilandelwe
UBume beNkampani okanye beTim yaBantu abasebenza ngeSicelo: Ubume basemthethweni bomenzi-sicelo
UkuMiswa kweeNkqubo: INani eliQingqiweyo lemiba eQulathiweyo yeNdawo; Ulwimi; IiNkqubo ezikhutshwa eMoyeni
Iimfuneko zobuchwepheshe.
Ifom yesicelo kunye nemimandla yeendawo ezipoposhwayo ziyinxenye yeGazethi kaRhulumente ekhutshelwa isimemo esingqalileyo sezicelo.

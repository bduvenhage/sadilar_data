Inkcazelo

Izilwanyana zingangeniswa elizweni kusetyenziswa imvume yethutyana yokungenisa elizweni ngenjongo:

yemiboniso, izibhengezo zentengiso, ukhuphiswano
yokusebenzisa amadlelo ngenjongo yoshishino
yokuhombisa ngezoshishino indawo yokutyebisa izilwanyana ezizakuxhelwa okanye ukuxhela ngokungqalileyo
yokunikela kumhlabi ozalisayo.

Izicelo ezingazaliswanga ngokupheleleyo ziya kuphindiswa kumenzi wesicelo yaye akukho xanduva luya kwamkelwa malunga nakuphi na ukuphazamiseka okunokwenzeka.

Kufuneka isicelo sinikelwe ku-Registrar of Animal Improvement  kwithuba leentsuku ezingama- 30 phambi kokwamkela into engeniswa elizweni.

Inkcazelo

I ofisi yamagosa endlela ikhupha ikopi yoxwebhu lemoto efana nesatifiketi sobhaliso, isatifiketi sokubasendleleni, icwecwe lephepha-mvume namanye amaxwebhu okanye imiqondiso elahlekileyo, eyonakele inkangeleko, etshatyalalisiweyo, okanye xa amanani noba, imifanekiso okanye iinkcukacha ezithile zingabonakali.

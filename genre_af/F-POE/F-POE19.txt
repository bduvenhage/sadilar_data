VIR RAYMOND
Wat sy verstand verloor het, voor hy dit regtens gekry het.
The protest against evil which is at the very core of metaphysical revolt is significant in this regard. It is not the suffering of a child which is repugnant in itself, but the fact that the suffering is not justified.
Albert Camus
